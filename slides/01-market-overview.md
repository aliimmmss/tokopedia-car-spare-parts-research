---

## Tokopedia Deep Dive

**Strengths:**
- Largest commercial vehicle parts catalog
- Power Merchant Pro benefits
- TopAds for visibility
- COD available

**Weaknesses:**
- Higher fees (3-7%)
- Strong competition
- Limited live selling features

**Best For:** Established brands, proven sellers

---

## Shopee Deep Dive

**Strengths:**
- Live selling tools (LIVE)
- Shopee Coins loyalty
- Free shipping vouchers
- Flash sales platform

**Weaknesses:**
- Race-to-bottom pricing
- Affiliate fees bite margins
- Faster but shallower catalog

**Best For:** Video content sellers, deals hunters

---

## TikTok Shop Deep Dive

**Strengths:**
- Viral discovery mechanism
- Video-first shopping
- Younger audience
- Low commission (1-5%)

**Weaknesses:**
- Small commercial vehicle audience
- Limited search
- New platform, evolving

**Best For:** Content creators, viral products

## Supply Chain Overview

```
Supplier/Distributor → Warehouse → Quality Check → List on 3 Platforms
                                          ↓
Customer Order → Processing → Ship (JNE/J&T/SiCepat) → Customer
                                          ↓
                                  Returns → Restock/Refund
```

**Key Partners:** JNE, J&T Express, SiCepat (COD enabled)
