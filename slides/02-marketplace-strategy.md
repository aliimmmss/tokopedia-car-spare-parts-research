# Multi-Marketplace Selling Strategy

## Commercial Vehicle Parts Distribution Strategy

---

## Why Multi-Marketplace?

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Reach** | 3x customer base vs single platform |
| **Risk** | Diversified if one platform changes rules |
| **Pricing Power** | Different price points per audience |
| **Data** | Cross-platform insights |
| **Growth** | Accelerate to 500+ SKU target |

**Example:** Same turbo listed at Rp 3.5M (Tokopedia), Rp 3.2M (Shopee), Rp 3.8M (TikTok bundle)

---

## Platform Selection Matrix

| Product Type | Tokopedia | Shopee | TikTok Shop |
|--------------|:---------:|:------:|:-----------:|
| Fast-moving filters | ✅ | ✅ | ⚠️ |
| High-value engine | ✅ | ⚠️ | ❌ |
| Trending/viral | ⚠️ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Service bundles | ✅ | ✅ | ⚠️ |
| OEM genuine | ✅ | ⚠️ | ❌ |
| Budget/aftermarket | ⚠️ | ✅ | ✅ |

✅ = Best fit | ⚠️ = Secondary | ❌ = Not recommended

---

## Tokopedia Strategy

**Store Optimization:**
- Complete profile 100% (logo, banner, about)
- Power Merchant Pro upgrade
- 50+ SKUs minimum for visibility

**Paid Advertising:**
- TopAds: Rp 5M-10M/month budget
- Target: oil filter, alternator keywords
- ROI target: 1:3 (Rp 1 ads = Rp 3 revenue)

**Trust Signals:**
- Verified badge
- COD available
- 4.8+ rating target
- 500+ reviews threshold

---

## Shopee Strategy

**Free Shipping Focus:**
- Offer voucher absorption (Rp 10-20K)
- Shopee Coins bonus (2%)
- Free shipping threshold: Rp 120K

**Live Selling:**
- Weekly LIVE sessions
- Product demonstration videos
- Flash sale countdowns

**Voucher Stacking:**
- Store voucher + Platform voucher
- 10-20% effective discount
- Minimum spend Rp 200K

---

## TikTok Shop Strategy

**Content Calendar:**
| Day | Content Type |
|-----|--------------|
| Mon | Turbo fails + replacement guide |
| Wed | Filter comparison OEM vs aftermarket |
| Fri | Customer testimonial videos |

**Video Format:**
- 15-60 seconds
- Product close-up + truck installation
- "Before/After" mechanic shots

**Affiliate Program:**
- 5-10% commission to creators
- Partner with mechanic influencers
- User-generated content push

---

## Product Listing Best Practices

**Title Formula:**
```
[BRAND] [PART NAME] [COMPATIBLE MODEL] [PART NUMBER] [CONDITION]
```
Example: "DENSO Alternator Hino J08E 24V 60A 27060-EW041 Original Baru"

**Images (5-9 per listing):**
1. Main product shot
2. Part number close-up
3. Compatibility chart
4. Installation photo
5. Shipping package
6. Warranty card

**Description:**
- 300+ words
- Bullet points for specs
- FAQ section
- Shipping times

## Pricing Per Platform

| Platform | Strategy | Single Unit | Bulk (10+) |
|----------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Tokopedia** | Premium | Rp 450K | Rp 380K |
| **Shopee** | Competitive | Rp 420K | Rp 360K |

**Dynamic Pricing Rules:**
- Monitor competitors weekly
