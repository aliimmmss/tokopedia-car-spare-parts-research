## Wholesale Pricing Strategy

| Tier | Discount | Volume | Customer Type |
|------|----------|--------|---------------|
| **Retail** | 0% | 1 unit | Individual owners |
| **Small Wholesale** | 10-15% | 5-20 units | Workshops, small fleets |
| **Large Wholesale** | 20-25% | 20-50 units | Regional distributors |
| **Fleet** | 25-35% | 50+ units | Major operators |
| **Strategic** | 30-40% | 100+ units | Exclusive partnerships |

**Payment Terms:**
- Retail: COD/Prepaid
- Wholesale: Net 7-14 days
- Fleet: Net 30-60 days (with deposit)

## Margin Analysis

| Channel | Retail Margin | Wholesale Margin | Net Result |
|---------|---------------|------------------|------------|
| **B2C Online** | 60-120% | - | 15-25% net |
| **Small Wholesale** | - | 30-40% | 15-20% net |
| **Fleet B2B** | - | 20-30% | 12-18% net |

**Key Insight:** Lower margin %, but higher absolute profit per transaction

**Example:**
- Retail: Rp 150K (60% margin) = Rp 90K profit
- Fleet (50 units): Rp 100K (30% margin) = Rp 30K × 50 = Rp 1.5M profit
