**Mitigation:**
- Start with minimal investment (Rp 1-2M/month — can pause anytime)
- Use 50 proven best-selling products (not random items)
- TopAds targets people **actively searching** for parts
- TikTok videos reach new audiences organically
- Even 5-10 orders/month covers our modest costs
- **No long-term commitment** — we can adjust or pause anytime

> "Worst case: we spend Rp 5M over 3 months and learn. Best case: we unlock a new revenue stream."

## Risk 2 — Shipping and Returns

**Risk:** "What if products get damaged or returned?"

**Mitigation:**
- We already know how to package parts properly (**30 years experience**)
- **Return rate for spare parts is very low** — typically 1-3%
- Marketplaces provide seller protection for shipping issues
- Require video unboxing for expensive items (over Rp 1M)
- Customer pays return shipping if they change their mind
- Insurance options through JNE/J&T for high-value items

**Packaging cost: Rp 2K-5K per order** — minimal compared to order value.

---

**Risk:** "Other sellers will undercut our prices."

- We have **30 years of product knowledge** — we advise, they just sell
- Fleet operators value **TRUST** over cheapest price. They buy in bulk and need reliability.
- We can offer **bulk/wholesale pricing** that dropshippers can't match
