# Restricted and Prohibited Products - Automotive Parts

## 1.1 General Status
Most common car spare parts are **PERMITTED**:
- Brake pads, Oil filters, Spark plugs, Wiper blades, Headlights, Suspension parts.

## 1.2 Potentially Restricted Items
| Category | Status | Requirements |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| Brake pads (asbestos) | ⚠️ RESTRICTED | Must be asbestos-free; need SNI certification |
| Car batteries | ⚠️ RESTRICTED | Hazardous material; special shipping |
| Engine oil/lubricants | ⚠️ RESTRICTED | Liquid restrictions; special packaging |
| Airbags | ❌ PROHIBITED | Safety hazard |
| Pressurized canisters | ❌ PROHIBITED | Explosive risk |

## 1.3 SNI (Standar Nasional Indonesia) Certification
**Mandatory for:** Brake pads and critical safety components.
- **Process:** Apply via BSN (Badan Standardisasi Nasional).
- **Timeline:** 2-4 months.
- **Validity:** 3 years.

## 1.4 Documentation
Maintain:
1. Invoice/Receipt from supplier.
2. Product certification copies.
3. Import documents (if applicable).
