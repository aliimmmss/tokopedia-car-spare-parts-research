# 📱 TikTok Shop Growth Strategy for Auto Parts

TikTok is not a search engine; it is a **discovery engine**. People don't go to TikTok to buy a spark plug; they see a video about a "stuttering engine" and then discover your spark plugs as the solution.

## 5.1 The Integration Workflow
1. **Sync:** Link your Tokopedia Seller account to the TikTok Shop Seller Center.
2. **Catalog:** Use the "Product Sync" feature to ensure prices and stock are identical.
3. **Payment:** All payments are processed via Tokopedia's secure gateway, reducing fraud.

## 5.2 High-Conversion Content Pillars
Stop making "ads" and start making "content." Use these three pillars:

### Pillar 1: The "Pain-Solution" (Problem Aware)
- **Hook:** "Is your car shaking when you brake?" or "Why is your fuel consumption increasing?"
- **Body:** Show the worn-out part (old brake pad) vs. your new part.
- **CTA:** "Check our bio for the correct part for your car."

### Pillar 2: The "Authenticity Test" (Trust Building)
- **Hook:** "Don't get scammed by fake NGK plugs!"
- **Body:** Side-by-side comparison of a fake vs. an original. Show the hologram, the font, and the packaging.
- **CTA:** "Buy original parts from a trusted seller. Link in bio."

### Pillar 3: The "ASMR/Satisfaction" (Visual Appeal)
- **Hook:** Fast-cut montage of a part being unboxed and installed.
- **Body:** Sound of the box opening, the "click" of a part fitting perfectly.
- **CTA:** "Upgrade your ride today."

## 5.3 Live Selling Tips for Auto Parts
Live streaming is the fastest way to clear inventory.
- **The "Compatibility Q&A":** The main goal of a live stream is to answer "Does this fit my [Car Model] [Year]?"
- **Live Demo:** Show the product in your hand. Let them see the quality, the weight, and the packaging.
- **Flash Sales:** Use TikTok's "Limited Time Voucher" to create urgency (e.g., "Only 10 sets of brake pads at this price for the next 30 minutes").

## 5.4 Affiliate Marketing Strategy (The "Expert" Network)
You don't need a million followers; you need 10 "Car Guys" who have 10,000 loyal followers each.

### How to recruit affiliates:
1. Search hashtags: `#TipsOtomotif`, `#ModifikasiMobil`, `#Bengkel`.
2. Look for creators who do "Review" or "Tutorial" content.
3. Offer a **tiered commission**:
   - **Standard:** 5-10% for general promotion.
   - **Expert:** 15% for creators who make full installation tutorials.
4. Provide a "Sample Kit" (e.g., a set of spark plugs) for them to test and review.

## 5.5 Indonesian Market Nuances
- **Peak Hours:** 19:00 - 22:00 WIB (when people are relaxing and scrolling).
- **Language:** Use a mix of formal and "slang" (e.g., using "Beres" or "Mantap").
- **Interaction:** Respond to every comment. In Indonesia, "chatting" before buying is a crucial part of the customer journey.
