# TikTok Shop Strategy

## 5.1 Integration
- Link Tokopedia account to TikTok Shop for synced catalogs and unified order management.

## 5.2 Content Strategy
- **Educational:** "5 Signs your brakes need replacement".
- **Comparison:** "Original vs Fake Oil Filters".
- **DIY:** "How to change spark plugs at home".
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds must address a car problem.

## 5.3 Affiliate Marketing
- Set commission between 5-20%.
- Find automotive micro-influencers using hashtags like #TipsOtomotif.
- Use a mix of product reviews and installation tutorials.
