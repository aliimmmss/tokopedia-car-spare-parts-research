## 1. BRAND-SPECIFIC AUTHORIZATION REQUIREMENTS

### 1.1 Hino Motors (Toyota Group)

**Official Channel:**
- **Authorized Dealer Network:** PT Hino Motors Sales Indonesia
- **Distribution:** Direct from factory (Karawang Plant)
- **Product Categories:** Genuine Hino, Hino Genuine Parts

**Authorization Requirements:**
| Requirement | Mandatory | Notes |
|-------------|-----------|-------|
| Official Dealer Contract | YES | From Hino branch office |
| Authorized Workshop Status | RECOMMENDED | For technical parts |
| Volume Commitment | YES | Minimum Rp 1B/year |
| Storage Facility Audit | YES | Must meet Hino standards |
| Training Certification | RECOMMENDED | Product knowledge |

**Evidence Documents:**
- Surat Keagenan Resmi (Authorized Agent Letter)
- NIB with KBLI matching
- NPWP perusahaan
- Foto gudang dan toko
- Rekening atas nama perusahaan

**Penalty for Unauthorized Selling:**
- Product takedown
- Shop suspension (3 days to permanent)
- Legal action possible for counterfeit claims

### 1.2 Isuzu Astra Motor Indonesia (IAMI)

**Official Channel:**
- **Principal:** PT Isuzu Astra Motor Indonesia
- **Distribution:** Distributor network + Authorized workshops
- **Parts Categories:** Genuine Isuzu, Isuzu Genuine Parts, NBC

**For Independent Sellers:**
Cannot sell "Genuine Isuzu" without authorization. Must use:
- "Aftermarket" label
- Compatible/Replacement designation
- Cannot use Isuzu logo in images

## 2. SELLING OEM PARTS WITHOUT DEALERSHIP STATUS

### 2.1 Legal Workarounds

**Method 1: Parallel Import**
- Source from authorized dealers overseas
- Import with proper documentation (PIB)
- Sell with "Import" designation
- Legal as long as parts are genuine

**Method 2: Supply Chain Resale**
- Buy from authorized workshops/distributors
- Keep supplier invoice
- Mark up and resell
- Legal (first sale doctrine applies)

**Method 3: Used/Refurbished Parts**
- "Copotan" from accident vehicles
- Reconditioned parts
- Clearly stated as used
- More lenient policies

### 2.2 Description Strategy

**FORBIDDEN words without authorization:**
- "Genuine [Brand]"
- "Original [Brand]"
- "Resmi"
- "Dealer"
- "Agen resmi"

**ALLOWED alternatives:**
- "Compatible with [Brand]"
- "Replacement for [Brand]"
- "Aftermarket"
- "Type [Brand]"

## 3. INVOICE DOCUMENTATION EXAMPLES

### 3.1 Valid Supplier Invoice Template

**Must Include:**
- Supplier business name and NPWP
- Invoice number and date
- Part number and description
- Quantity
- Unit price and total
- Stamp/live signature

### 3.2 Import Documentation (PIB)

**Required Elements:**
- Shipper name and address
- Consignee (your business)
- HS Code (tarif pos)
- Part descriptions
- CIF value in USD
- Import duty and tax (PPH, PPN)

## 4. COUNTERFEIT (KW/PALSU) PARTS PROBLEM

### 4.1 Identifying Counterfeit Parts

**Visual Indicators:**
| Aspect | Genuine | KW/Counterfeit |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| Box Quality | Thick cardboard, glossy print | Thin box, dull/faded |
| Logo Printing | Sharp, embossed | Blurry, flat print |
| Part Number | Laser etched, consistent | Printed, irregular |
| Serial Number | Unique, verifiable online | Generic or missing |
| Packaging Seal | Holographic, tamper-proof | Plain tape, easily opened |
| Weight | Matches OEM spec | Often lighter |
| Surface Finish | Smooth, precise machining | Rough, irregular |

**Price Red Flags:**
- Below 50 percent of market price
- "Too good to be true" deals
- Bulk pricing suspiciously low
- "Clearance sale" from unknown source

### 4.2 Proving Authenticity

**For Marketplaces:**
1. **Purchase Documentation**
   - Supplier invoice with NPWP
   - Import documents if applicable
   - Authorized dealer receipt

2. **Product Documentation**
   - Manufacturer certificates
   - Test reports
   - MSDS (for applicable parts)

3. **Verification Channels**
   - Official brand hotline
   - Website serial check
   - Authorized distributor confirmation

4. **Physical Verification**
   - Side-by-side comparison photos
   - Magnified detail shots
   - Weight vs official spec comparison

## 5. BRAND PROTECTION STRATEGY

### 5.1 Creating Brand Independence

**Private Label Approach:**
1. Source quality aftermarket parts
2. Create own brand name (e.g., "TrukPro", "Maju Diesel")
3. Custom packaging with your brand
4. Build reputation on quality, not OEM association

**Benefits:**
- No authorization needed
- Higher margins
- Less price competition
- Customer loyalty to your brand

### 5.2 Authorized Partnership Path

**Steps to Become Authorized:**
1. **Legal Entity Setup**
   - PT (Perseroan Terbatas) formation
   - NIB registration
   - VAT (PKP) registration
   - Business bank account

2. **Facility Requirements**
   - Minimum 200m2 warehouse
   - Office space
   - Security system
   - Inventory management system

3. **Financial Requirements**
   - Audited statements
   - Bank guarantee
   - Working capital proof (Rp 500M+)
   - Credit references

4. **Application Process**
   - Submit to brand directly
   - Interview with regional manager
   - Site visit by brand representative
   - Training program attendance
   - Sign multi-year agreement

**Timeline and Costs:**
| Brand | Application Fee | Inventory Requirement | Timeline |
|-------|-------------------|----------------------|----------|
| Hino | Rp 50M | Rp 1B | 3-6 months |
| Isuzu | Rp 25M | Rp 500M | 2-4 months |
| Mitsubishi | Rp 30M | Rp 750M | 3-5 months |

## 7. SPECIFIC BRAND GUIDELINES

- PT Denso Indonesia (Karawang)
- Aftermarket via PT Astra Denso Indonesia

**Authorization Tiers:**
| Tier | Status | Requirements |
|------|--------|--------------|

| Independent | No authorization | Can sell as "compatible" |

**Key Part Number Series:**
- Fuel injectors: 095000-xxxx
- Spark plugs: IK series
- Fuel pumps: 294000-xxxx

### 7.2 Bosch Germany

**Indonesia Operation:**
- PT Robert Bosch (Tangerang)
- Aftermarket: Bosch Automotive Aftermarket

**Requires Authorization:**
- Brake components
- Sensors (OEM)
- Fuel injectors (some)

**Open to Independent Sales:**
- Tools
- Generic parts (brake fluid)
- Some aftermarket brake pads

### 7.3 Garrett Turbo (Honeywell)

**Distribution:** PT Turbo Solutions Indonesia

**Strict Authorization Required:**
- Garrett branded turbos
- Original equipment parts
- "Meleq" designation

**Aftermarket Options (No Authorization):**
- Turbo repair kits
- Compatible replacement turbos
- "Turbo type Garrett"

**Distribution Structure:**
- **Principal:** PT Krama Yudha Tiga Berlian

- **Categories:** Original, Genuine Parts, Value Parts

**Requirements for Authorization:**
- Rp 250M minimum stock
- Workshop facility (for commercial)
- Geographic exclusivity commitment
- Sales target agreement (Rp 2B/year)
