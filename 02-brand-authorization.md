# Brand Authorization & IP Rights

## 2.1 Selling Branded Parts (NGK, Bosch, Denso, etc.)
You **CAN** sell genuine branded products without being an official authorized dealer (Parallel Imports/Gray Market).

### Requirements for Non-Authorized Sellers:
- Products MUST be 100% authentic.
- Must provide proof of legitimate source (Supplier Invoices) if challenged by the brand.

## 2.2 Authorized Reseller vs. Gray Market
- **Authorized:** Has Official Appointment Letter. Benefits: Official badge, brand assets.
- **Gray Market:** Genuine products from unofficial channels. Allowed, but riskier if the brand files an IP complaint.

## 2.3 OEM & Unbranded Parts
- **Permitted.** 
- **Labeling:** Clearly mark as "OEM" or "Aftermarket".
- **Restriction:** Do NOT use brand names in the title for unbranded parts (e.g., don't say "NGK-style").
