# 🏷️ Brand Authorization and IP Rights Guide

One of the biggest challenges for automotive sellers in Indonesia is the "Original vs. KW" (Fake) battle. Tokopedia is aggressive in protecting Intellectual Property (IP) to maintain buyer trust.

## 2.1 The "Authorized Dealer" Myth
A common misconception is that you *must* be an official dealer to sell brands like NGK, Bosch, or Denso. **This is false.**

### The Concept of Parallel Imports (Gray Market)
Tokopedia allows the sale of genuine products purchased from authorized distributors, even if you aren't a "certified dealer." This is known as parallel importing.

**The Gold Rule:** As long as the product is **GENUINE**, you can sell it. If the product is **FAKE**, you will be banned regardless of your "dealer" status.

## 2.2 How to Prove Authenticity (The Defense Strategy)
If a brand owner (e.g., Bosch Indonesia) files a "Counterfeit" report against your shop, Tokopedia will freeze the listing and ask for proof.

### Required Proof Documents:
1. **The Paper Trail:** A complete chain of invoices from the Brand $ightarrow$ Main Distributor $ightarrow$ Wholesaler $ightarrow$ You.
2. **Matching Batch Numbers:** Photos of the batch/lot numbers on the product packaging that match the supplier's invoice.
3. **Import Documents:** For imported parts, the **PIB (Pemberitahuan Impor Barang)** document is the ultimate proof of legality.

## 2.3 Selling OEM vs. Branded Parts
"OEM" (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts are those made by the company that supplied the car manufacturer, but sold without the car brand's logo.

### Labeling Strategy to Avoid IP Bans:
| WRONG (Risk of Ban) | RIGHT (Safe) |
| :--- | :--- |
| "NGK Style Spark Plug" | "Premium Iridium Spark Plug (OEM)" |
| "Compatible with Bosch Filter" | "High-Efficiency Oil Filter for [Car Model]" |
| "Fake Denso Wipers" | "Aftermarket Wiper Blades - High Grade" |

**Pro Tip:** Never use the word "Style" or "Grade" next to a brand name. This is a red flag for IP bots.

## 2.4 Steps to Become an Official Authorized Dealer
If you want the "Official Store" badge and full brand protection, follow these steps:
1. **Contact the Brand Representative:** Reach out to the brand's Indonesian office (e.g., NGK Indonesia).
2. **Submit a Business Profile:** Provide your KTP, NPWP (Tax ID), and proof of shop location/warehouse.
3. **Commit to Volume:** Brands usually require a minimum monthly purchase commitment.
4. **SLA Agreement:** Sign an agreement to maintain pricing (MAP - Minimum Advertised Price) and service standards.
5. **Submit to Tokopedia:** Once you have the "Appointment Letter," upload it to Tokopedia's Official Store application.

## 2.5 Practical Tips for the Indonesian Market
- **Anti-Fake Content:** Create a video showing "How to spot a fake NGK plug." This positions you as an expert and proves your stock is original.
- **Avoid "KW" Terminology:** Never use "KW Super" or "KW 1" in your listings. Even if buyers use these terms, using them in your description admits that the product is not original, which violates Tokopedia's Terms of Service.
