    2. SEASONAL DEMAND SPIKES¶

    A. Mudik / Lebaran Season (Peak: March-April)¶

    Metric Data
    Sales Increase 20%+ for direct-to-consumer sales
    Golden Moment Before mudik + After mudik (return trip)
    Duration Extends several weeks post-Lebaran
    Source PT Astra Otoparts (GridOto interview)

    Top Products During Mudik: - Brake pads & brake shoes (safety check before long trip) - Oil filter + engine oil (pre-trip service) - Wiper blades (rainy season overlap) - Suspension parts (heavy load preparation) - Tires (safety replacement)

    Strategy: Stock up 1 month before Ramadan. Run promos 2 weeks before estimated mudik dates.

    B. Year-End Holiday (Peak: November-December)¶

    Metric Data
    RSI Growth 4.3% yoy (Oct 2025: 219.7 index)
    Key Drivers Christmas, New Year, Year-End Bonuses
    Spare Parts Sales +7.5% yoy (July 2025 data, Bank Indonesia)

    Top Products During Year-End: - Vehicle accessories (gift purchases) - Premium replacement parts (bonus spending) - Car care products (polish, detailing) - Battery replacements (rainy season overlap)

    Strategy: Premium products sell better — consumers have more budget. Bundle accessories with maintenance items.

    C. Rainy Season (Peak: November-February)¶

    Metric Data
    Key Categories Wipers, AC/Cabin filters, Brakes
    Manufacturer Response Toyota, Mitsubishi, Suzuki run rainy season promos

    Top Products During Rainy Season: | Product | Reason | Promo Activity | |---------|--------|----------------| | Wiper Blades | Visibility safety | Toyota: 10% discount | | Cabin/AC Filter | Humidity, mold prevention | Bosch: active marketing | | Brake Pads | Wet road braking | Mitsubishi: Rainy Campaign | | Tires | Wet traction safety | Various tire promos | | Car covers | Parking protection | Seasonal demand |

    Strategy: Stock wipers and cabin filters from October. Partner with brands running official rainy season campaigns.

    D. Beginning of School Year (Peak: July-August)¶

    Metric Data
    Consumer Segment Parents, students (motorcycle focus)
    Key Drivers New semester, student transportation needs
    Spare Parts Focus Two-wheeler maintenance

    Top Products: - Motorcycle spare parts (brake pads, chains, tires) - Helmet accessories - Basic car maintenance (family vehicles for school runs)

    Note: This season has LESS impact on commercial vehicle parts. Focus on consumer/motorcycle segment.

    3. COMMERCIAL VEHICLE PARTS DEMAND TRENDS¶

    EURO 4 Transition Impact¶

    Status Details
    Implementation Gradual since 2021, national mandate ongoing
    Mining Sector Enforcement tightened for foreign trucks
    Market Effect New demand for EURO 4 specific parts

    EURO 4 Specific Parts (Growing Demand):

    Product Price Range Growth Driver
    Common Rail Injector Rp 2.3M - Rp 7M EURO 4 engine requirement
    Fuel Filter (Common Rail) Rp 135K - Rp 4.35M High-pressure fuel system
    Diesel Particulate Filter Premium Emission compliance
    EGR Valve Components Medium-High Emission control

    Commercial Vehicle Market Context: - Heavy-duty vehicle parts aftermarket: USD 92.73B in 2025 (global) - Indonesian aftermarket: USD 4.82B in 2025, growing to USD 7.59B by 2034 - CAGR: 4.78% - Vehicle parks expanding 5-6% annually until 2030

    EURO 4 Opportunity Assessment:

    Factor Impact
    Fleet Renewal Older trucks being replaced with EURO 4 models
    Parts Complexity More expensive, specialized components
    Competition LOWER — specialized knowledge required
    Margin HIGHER — technical products, fewer sellers
    Customer Loyalty HIGH — fleet operators return to trusted suppliers
