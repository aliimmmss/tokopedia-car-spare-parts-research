    Brand Authorization Requirements for Commercial Vehicle Parts¶

    Indonesia Market - Official Dealer vs Independent Seller Guide¶

    Scope: Tokopedia, Shopee, TikTok Shop brand policies
    Focus: Hino, Isuzu, Mitsubishi Fuso, Toyota, Denso, Bosch, Garrett, NPR
    Last Updated: April 8, 2026

    1. BRAND-SPECIFIC AUTHORIZATION REQUIREMENTS¶

    1.1 Hino Motors (Toyota Group)¶

    Official Channel: - Authorized Dealer Network: PT Hino Motors Sales Indonesia - Distribution: Direct from factory (Karawang Plant) - Product Categories: Genuine Hino, Hino Genuine Parts

    Authorization Requirements: | Requirement | Mandatory | Notes | |-------------|-----------|-------| | Official Dealer Contract | YES | From Hino branch office | | Authorized Workshop Status | RECOMMENDED | For technical parts | | Volume Commitment | YES | Minimum Rp 1B/year | | Storage Facility Audit | YES | Must meet Hino standards | | Training Certification | RECOMMENDED | Product knowledge |

    Evidence Documents: - Surat Keagenan Resmi (Authorized Agent Letter) - NIB with KBLI matching - NPWP perusahaan - Foto gudang dan toko - Rekening atas nama perusahaan

    Penalty for Unauthorized Selling: - Product takedown - Shop suspension (3 days to permanent) - Legal action possible for counterfeit claims

    1.2 Isuzu Astra Motor Indonesia (IAMI)¶

    Official Channel: - Principal: PT Isuzu Astra Motor Indonesia - Distribution: Distributor network + Authorized workshops - Parts Categories: Genuine Isuzu, Isuzu Genuine Parts, NBC

    For Independent Sellers: Cannot sell "Genuine Isuzu" without authorization. Must use: - "Aftermarket" label - Compatible/Replacement designation - Cannot use Isuzu logo in images

    2. SELLING OEM PARTS WITHOUT DEALERSHIP STATUS¶

    2.1 Legal Workarounds¶

    Method 1: Parallel Import - Source from authorized dealers overseas - Import with proper documentation (PIB) - Sell with "Import" designation - Legal as long as parts are genuine

    Method 2: Supply Chain Resale - Buy from authorized workshops/distributors - Keep supplier invoice - Mark up and resell - Legal (first sale doctrine applies)

    Method 3: Used/Refurbished Parts - "Copotan" from accident vehicles - Reconditioned parts - Clearly stated as used - More lenient policies

    2.2 Description Strategy¶

    FORBIDDEN words without authorization: - "Genuine [Brand]" - "Original [Brand]" - "Resmi" - "Dealer" - "Agen resmi"

    ALLOWED alternatives: - "Compatible with [Brand]" - "Replacement for [Brand]" - "Aftermarket" - "Type [Brand]"

    3. INVOICE DOCUMENTATION EXAMPLES¶

    3.1 Valid Supplier Invoice Template¶

    Must Include: - Supplier business name and NPWP - Invoice number and date - Part number and description - Quantity - Unit price and total - Stamp/live signature

    3.2 Import Documentation (PIB)¶

    Required Elements: - Shipper name and address - Consignee (your business) - HS Code (tarif pos) - Part descriptions - CIF value in USD - Import duty and tax (PPH, PPN)

    4. COUNTERFEIT (KW/PALSU) PARTS PROBLEM¶

    4.1 Identifying Counterfeit Parts¶

    Visual Indicators: | Aspect | Genuine | KW/Counterfeit | |--------|---------|----------------| | Box Quality | Thick cardboard, glossy print | Thin box, dull/faded | | Logo Printing | Sharp, embossed | Blurry, flat print | | Part Number | Laser etched, consistent | Printed, irregular | | Serial Number | Unique, verifiable online | Generic or missing | | Packaging Seal | Holographic, tamper-proof | Plain tape, easily opened | | Weight | Matches OEM spec | Often lighter | | Surface Finish | Smooth, precise machining | Rough, irregular |

    Price Red Flags: - Below 50 percent of market price - "Too good to be true" deals - Bulk pricing suspiciously low - "Clearance sale" from unknown source

    4.2 Proving Authenticity¶

    For Marketplaces: 1. Purchase Documentation - Supplier invoice with NPWP - Import documents if applicable - Authorized dealer receipt

    1. Product Documentation
    2. Manufacturer certificates
    3. Test reports

    4. MSDS (for applicable parts)

    5. Verification Channels

    6. Official brand hotline
    7. Website serial check

    8. Authorized distributor confirmation

    9. Physical Verification

    10. Side-by-side comparison photos
    11. Magnified detail shots
    12. Weight vs official spec comparison

    5. BRAND PROTECTION STRATEGY¶

    5.1 Creating Brand Independence¶

    Private Label Approach: 1. Source quality aftermarket parts 2. Create own brand name (e.g., "TrukPro", "Maju Diesel") 3. Custom packaging with your brand 4. Build reputation on quality, not OEM association

    Benefits: - No authorization needed - Higher margins - Less price competition - Customer loyalty to your brand

    5.2 Authorized Partnership Path¶

    Steps to Become Authorized: 1. Legal Entity Setup - PT (Perseroan Terbatas) formation - NIB registration - VAT (PKP) registration - Business bank account

    1. Facility Requirements
    2. Minimum 200m2 warehouse
    3. Office space
    4. Security system

    5. Inventory management system

    6. Financial Requirements

    7. Audited statements
    8. Bank guarantee
    9. Working capital proof (Rp 500M+)

    10. Credit references

    11. Application Process

    12. Submit to brand directly
    13. Interview with regional manager
    14. Site visit by brand representative
    15. Training program attendance
    16. Sign multi-year agreement

    Timeline and Costs: | Brand | Application Fee | Inventory Requirement | Timeline | |-------|-------------------|----------------------|----------| | Hino | Rp 50M | Rp 1B | 3-6 months | | Isuzu | Rp 25M | Rp 500M | 2-4 months | | Mitsubishi | Rp 30M | Rp 750M | 3-5 months |

    7. SPECIFIC BRAND GUIDELINES¶

    7.1 Denso Japan¶

    Indonesia Presence: - PT Denso Indonesia (Karawang) - Aftermarket via PT Astra Denso Indonesia

    Authorization Tiers: | Tier | Status | Requirements | |------|--------|--------------| | Authorized Distributor | Full rights | Warehouse, Rp 1B+ stock, contract | | Authorized Reseller | Limited | Sourced from authorized dist. | | Independent | No authorization | Can sell as "compatible" |

    Key Part Number Series: - Fuel injectors: 095000-xxxx - Spark plugs: IK series - Fuel pumps: 294000-xxxx

    7.2 Bosch Germany¶

    Indonesia Operation: - PT Robert Bosch (Tangerang) - Aftermarket: Bosch Automotive Aftermarket

    Requires Authorization: - Brake components - Sensors (OEM) - Fuel injectors (some)

    Open to Independent Sales: - Tools - Generic parts (brake fluid) - Some aftermarket brake pads

    7.3 Garrett Turbo (Honeywell)¶

    Distribution: PT Turbo Solutions Indonesia

    Strict Authorization Required: - Garrett branded turbos - Original equipment parts - "Meleq" designation

    Aftermarket Options (No Authorization): - Turbo repair kits - Compatible replacement turbos - "Turbo type Garrett"

    7.4 Mitsubishi Fuso (PT KTB)¶

    Distribution Structure: - Principal: PT Krama Yudha Tiga Berlian - Network: Dealer + Sub-dealer - Categories: Original, Genuine Parts, Value Parts

    Requirements for Authorization: - Rp 250M minimum stock - Workshop facility (for commercial) - Geographic exclusivity commitment - Sales target agreement (Rp 2B/year)
