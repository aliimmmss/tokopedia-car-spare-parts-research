    SECTION 7: SPECIAL NOTES¶

    7.1 Euro 4 Considerations¶

    • Euro 4 vehicles (2018+) require:
    • Fuel water separator
    • MAF/MAP sensors
    • Crankshaft position sensor
    • Specialized fuel filters
    • These parts are more expensive and less available
    • Consider stocking for fleet customers

    7.2 Battery Configuration¶

    • Light Trucks: Single N150 or N120 (12V)
    • Medium Trucks: 2x N200 (24V system)
    • Heavy Trucks/Bus: 2x N200 or N250 (24V system)
    • Typical replacement: 1-2 years

    7.3 ECU/ECM Warning¶

    • ECU replacement requires:
    • Dealer programming
    • VIN-specific coding
    • Security code pairing
    • Not recommended for self-service
    • Consider remanufactured units for cost savings

    7.4 Radiator Options¶

    • New Complete: Rp 2.5M - Rp 4M
    • Core Replacement: Rp 1.5M - Rp 2.5M
    • Recoring Service: Rp 1M - Rp 1.8M
