    Focus: Spare Parts & Automotive Components | Commercial & Consumer Vehicles
    Target: Tokopedia sellers expanding to TikTok Shop Indonesia

    1.3 Payment Flow (Tokopedia Integration)¶

    Customer Orders on TikTok Shop
        ↓
Payment Processed via Tokopedia Gateway (invisible to customer)
        ↓
Seller Receives order in Tokopedia Seller Center
        ↓
Seller Ships via Tokopedia-integrated couriers
        ↓
Customer receives product and can review on both platforms
        ↓
Payment released to seller (T+3 to T+7 days)

    Key Integration Benefits: - Single inventory management (stock syncs automatically) - Unified chat system (Tokopedia chat appears on TikTok) - Combined reviews boost credibility - Simplified payment reconciliation - Shared return/refund processing

    🎥 SECTION 2: PRODUCT VIDEO CONTENT STRATEGY¶

    2.1 Video Content Framework for Spare Parts¶

    TikTok is not a search engine; it is a discovery engine. People don't go to TikTok to buy a spark plug; they see a video about a "stuttering engine" and then discover your spark plugs as the solution.

    Content Format Specifications¶

    Format Length Best For Hook Placement
    Quick Tips 15-30 seconds Simple parts, comparisons 0-3 seconds
    Tutorial 45-90 seconds Installation guides 0-5 seconds
    Story 60-120 seconds Before/after transformations 0-10 seconds
    Live Preview 5-30 minutes Product showcases, Q&A Opening statement
    ASMR/Unboxing 30-60 seconds Premium parts, quality focus Sound/visual hook

    2.2 The Three High-Conversion Content Pillars¶

    Pillar 1: The "Pain-Solution" (Problem Aware)¶

    Target: Vehicle owners experiencing specific problems

    Video Structure: - Hook (0-3s): Visual + audio that immediately identifies the problem - Agitation (3-10s): Amplify the pain point (cost of dealer, danger of ignoring) - Solution (10-25s): Show the product + quick demo - CTA (25-30s): Link in bio + urgency

    Example Scripts:

    Spark Plug Script: 

    HOOK: "Is your car shaking when you brake? Or jerking when you accelerate?"
AGITATION: "That's a failing spark plug. Ignore it and you're looking at catalytic converter damage - Rp 15 juta+ repair."
SOLUTION: "These NGK Iridium plugs. 15-minute install. Rp 180 ribu."
CTA: "Link in bio for compatibility check."

    Brake Pad Script: 

    HOOK: "Hear that grinding sound when braking? That's metal on metal."
AGITATION: "Dealer quoted Rp 8 juta? Try Rp 1.2 juta DIY."
SOLUTION: [Show old vs new pads] "Brembo pads + 30-minute install."
CTA: "Shop link in bio - free shipping today."

    What to Film: - Close-ups of worn/damaged parts - Side-by-side comparison (old vs new) - Cross-sections showing wear - Quick installation snippets - Before/after performance tests

    Pillar 2: The "Authenticity Test" (Trust Building)¶

    Target: Skeptical buyers worried about fake/imitation parts

    Video Structure: - Hook (0-5s): "Don't get scammed by fake [brand] parts!" - Reveal (5-20s): Show detailed comparison point by point - Education (20-40s): Teach authentication markers - Proof (40-50s): Show genuine packaging/supplier invoices - CTA (50-60s): "Only buy from authorized sellers. Link in bio."

    Authentication Points to Film: - Holographic stickers (angle changes) - Laser-etched part numbers - Packaging quality differences - Font and printing quality - Weight differences (use scale) - QR code verification process

    What to Film: - Macro shots of holograms and logos - Split-screen comparisons - UV light tests (if applicable) - QR code scanning in real-time - Packaging material comparison - Supplier documentation (blurred for privacy)

    Pillar 3: The "ASMR/Satisfaction" (Visual Appeal)¶

    Target: Scrollers who engage with satisfying content

    Video Structure: - Hook (0-3s): Fast visual or distinctive sound - Satisfying Content (3-40s): Unboxing, install, or test - Result (40-50s): Clean finish or working system - CTA (50-55s): "Upgrade your ride. Link in bio."

    What to Film: - Unboxing high-quality packaging - Revealing pristine parts - Perfect fit installation - Tightening torque sequences - First start-up after install - Dashboard lights clearing - Clean workspace transformations

    Audio Priorities: - Box opening sounds - Plastic wrap crinkling - Metal-to-metal contact - Ratchet clicking - Engine starting smoothly - Satisfying "click" of proper fit

    2.3 Video Production Checklist¶

    Essential Equipment¶

    • Smartphone with 1080p+ camera (iPhone 12+, Samsung S20+, etc.)
    • Ring light or softbox (for consistent lighting)
    • Tripod or phone holder (stable shots)
    • Lapel mic or phone mic with windscreen (clear audio)
    • White/gray backdrop (clean professional look)
    • Macro lens attachment (for detail shots - optional)

    Technical Settings¶

    • Resolution: 1080x1920 (9:16 vertical format)
    • Frame rate: 60fps for action, 30fps for tutorials
    • Lighting: 5000-6500K color temperature
    • Export: MP4, H.264 codec

    📺 SECTION 3: LIVE SELLING STRATEGY¶

    3.1 Live Stream Setup for Spare Parts¶

    Live streaming is the fastest way to clear inventory and build trust.

    Weekly Live Schedule Template¶

    Day Time Theme Duration Target
    Monday 19:00-20:30 FAQ & Compatibility 90 min Answering questions
    Wednesday 20:00-21:30 Product Showcase 90 min New arrivals
    Friday 19:00-21:00 Flash Sale 120 min Volume sales
    Sunday 15:00-16:30 Installation Tutorial 90 min Education

    3.2 Live Content Pillars¶

    A. The "Compatibility Q&A" Format¶

    Purpose: Answer "Does this fit my [Car Model]?"

    Flow: 1. Opening (5 min): "Drop your car model + year in comments, I'll check compatibility live" 2. Q&A Session (60-75 min): Go through comments, pull parts, show fitment 3. Flash Offers (15 min): Limited-time bundles for engaged viewers 4. Closing (5 min): Reminder to follow, next live schedule

    What You Need: - Full compatibility database (printed or digital) - Physical samples of top 20 parts - Measuring tools (calipers, rulers) - Sample vehicles or detailed photos

    B. The "Installation Workshop" Format¶

    Purpose: Build authority and demonstrate quality

    Flow: 1. Introduction (10 min): Today's project overview 2. Preparation (15 min): Tools needed, safety first 3. Step-by-step (45-60 min): Actual installation demonstration 4. Testing (10 min):" Verify installation success 5. Q&A (10 min):** Answer live questions

    Popular Workshop Topics: - Brake pad replacement (most common) - Oil and filter change - Spark plug replacement - Air filter installation - Headlight bulb upgrade - Basic diagnostic with OBD reader

    C. The "Clearance Flash Sale" Format¶

    Purpose: Move inventory quickly

    Tactics: - Countdown timers on screen - Limited quantities announced - Bundle deals ("3 for Rp 199k") - Free shipping thresholds - Exclusive live-only prices - "Next 5 orders get free gift"

    3.3 Live Stream Best Practices¶

    Before Going Live: - Test internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps upload) - Set up good lighting (face well-lit, product visible) - Prepare parts within arm's reach - Charge phone to 100% + have backup power - Create promotional materials (prices on cards)

    During Live: - Engagement Rule: Acknowledge every comment by name - Energy: Maintain high energy - viewers join/leave constantly - Repetition: Repeat key info every 5 minutes (new viewers joining) - Visuals: Show product from multiple angles - Urgency: Create genuine scarcity and time limits

    After Live: - Send thank you messages to top engagers - Process orders immediately - Reply to missed comments - Schedule next live within 48 hours - Analyze metrics (views, engagement, sales)

    🤝 SECTION 4: AFFILIATE & INFLUENCER STRATEGY¶

    4.1 The "Expert Network" Approach¶

    You don't need a million followers; you need 10 "Car Guys" who have 10,000 loyal followers each.

    4.2 Finding Affiliates¶

    Search Hashtags: - #TipsOtomotif (automotive tips) - #ModifikasiMobil (car modifications) - #Bengkel (workshop) - #ReviewOtomotif - #Sparepart - #OliMobil

    Profile Criteria: - 5,000-50,000 followers (micro-influencers) - Automotive content >70% of posts - Engagement rate >3% - Indonesian audience >80% - Consistent posting (3+/week)

    4.3 Commission Structure¶

    Tier Commission Requirements Support
    Bronze 5-8% 1-5 sales/month Basic product info
    Silver 10-12% 6-15 sales/month Free samples
    Gold 15-18% 16-30 sales/month Free samples + Exclusive previews
    Platinum 20-25% 30+ sales/month Co-branded content, wholesale pricing

    4.4 Content Collaboration Types¶

    Type 1: Product Review (One-time) - You send free product - Creator makes review video - Uses your affiliate link in bio - Timeline: 1-2 weeks

    Type 2: Tutorial Series (Ongoing) - Monthly content collaboration - Installation guides featuring your parts - Brand mention in every video - Timeline: 3-month contract

    Type 3: Brand Ambassador (Exclusive) - Exclusive partnership - Mention competitor restrictions - Monthly content quota - Product line endorsement - Timeline: 6-12 months

    ⚠️ SECTION 7: COMPLIANCE & PROHIBITED ITEMS¶

    7.1 TikTok Shop Policy Alignment¶

    Prohibited Auto Parts (Cannot Sell): - Used/recycled airbags and components - Counterfeit branded parts (fake NGK, Bosch, etc.) - Odometer manipulation tools - License plate covers/obscurers - Radar detectors (police scanner type) - Illegal exhaust modifications - Fuel line components that bypass emissions - Stolen/recovered parts without documentation

    Restricted (Need Documentation): - OEM branded parts (proof of authenticity required) - Performance modifications (may need certification) - Hazardous materials (MSDS required for fluids) - Electronics with radio transmitters (postel certification)

    7.2 SNI Certification Requirements¶

    Product Category SNI Required Certificate Validity Body
    Brake Pads Yes - SNI 09-0944-2003 4 years KAN accredited
    Helmets Yes - SNI 1811:2007 4 years LSPro
    Seat Belts Yes - SNI 06-2481-1991 4 years LSPro
    Lighting Yes - varies by type 4 years LSPro
    Batteries Yes - SNI 06-4752-1998 4 years LSPro
    Tires Yes - SNI 06-0098-2006 4 years LSPro
    Filters No N/A N/A
    Lubricants No (but quality standards apply) N/A N/A

    7.3 Content Guidelines¶

    Prohibited in TikTok Content: - ❌ Showing illegal installations - ❌ Speeding/dangerous driving demonstrations - ❌ Tampering with emissions control systems - ❌ Counterfeit comparisons using trademarked logos ( risky) - ❌ Unsafe workshop practices - ❌ False claims ("increase horsepower by 50%")

    Required Disclaimers: - "Professional installation recommended" for complex parts - "Results may vary" for performance claims - "Check compatibility before purchase" - Age restrictions for tools (18+)
