    🏁 Implementation Action Checklist: 90-Day Launch Plan¶

    Use this checklist as your roadmap from "Zero" to "First Sale" to "Sustainable Growth"

    Target: Tokopedia, Shopee, and TikTok Shop multi-platform launch
    Focus: Commercial vehicle spare parts (trucks, buses) and consumer auto parts
    Timeline: 12 weeks (90 days)

    📋 QUICK REFERENCE: PHASE OVERVIEW¶

    Phase Timeline Focus Key Deliverables
    Phase 1 Week 1-2 Pre-Launch Foundation Legal, compliance, supplier agreements
    Phase 2 Week 3-4 Soft Launch Platform setup, initial listings, testing
    Phase 3 Week 5-8 Growth Phase Marketing activation, live selling, affiliates
    Phase 4 Week 9-12 Scale Phase Optimization, wholesale outreach, automation

    PHASE 1: PRE-LAUNCH FOUNDATION (Weeks 1-2)¶

    Week 1: Legal & Business Setup¶

    Day 1-2: Business Documentation¶

    • NPWP Registration (if not already done)
    • Visit: pajak.go.id
    • Required for seller verification

    • Timeline: 1-3 business days

    • Business Verification Documents

    • Scan KTP (pemilik)
    • Scan KK (if required)
    • Recent photo (for verification)

    • Bank account in business name or owner's name

    • Bank Account Setup

    • Recomendation: BCA, Mandiri, or BRI (fastest verification)
    • Ensure name matches NPWP
    • Prepare bank statement (last 3 months if business account)

    Day 3-4: Supplier & Inventory Agreements¶

    • Supplier Audits
    • Create supplier database spreadsheet
    • Document: Nama supplier, kontak, lokasi, terms pembayaran
    • Request certificate of authenticity for OEM parts

    • Photograph supplier location/warehouse

    • Initial Stock Ordering

    • Calculate minimum viable stock: 10-20 units per SKU for launch
    • Prioritize fast-moving items (refer to File 09-demand-analysis.md)
    • Negotiate consignment terms where possible
    • Set up inventory tracking system (Google Sheets minimum)

    Day 5-7: Compliance & Certification¶

    • SNI Certification Check (File 01-restricted-products.md)
    • Brake pads: Must have SNI 09-0944-2003
    • Filters: No SNI required (brand dependent)
    • Lubricants: No SNI but need quality standards

    • Electrical: SNI varies by component

    • Prohibited Items Audit

    • Review all planned products against platform lists
    • Remove any counterfeit or gray market items
    • Document serial numbers for OEM parts
    • Create authenticity verification process

    Week 1 Budget: Rp 500,000 - 2,000,000 (licenses, initial stock deposit, documentation)

    Week 2: Technical Infrastructure¶

    Day 8-10: Seller Account Creation¶

    SHOPEE Setup: - [ ] Create account at seller.shopee.co.id - [ ] Complete verification (KTP + phone) - [ ] Apply for Star Seller (if meets criteria) - [ ] Link bank account - [ ] Configure shop settings - [ ] Download Shopee Seller app

    TIKTOK SHOP Setup: - [ ] Create account at seller-id.tiktok.com - [ ] Link with existing Tokopedia account (if integrated) - [ ] Complete business verification - [ ] Link bank account - [ ] Download TikTok Shop Seller app

    Day 11-14: Workspace Preparation¶

    Packaging Station: - [ ] Stock packaging materials: - [ ] Bubble wrap (3 roll minimum - various sizes) - [ ] Poly mailers (3 sizes: small, medium, large) - [ ] Corrugated boxes (3-4 sizes for different parts) - [ ] Fragile stickers - [ ] Packaging tape (dispenser type) - [ ] Weighing scale (digital, minimum 10kg capacity) - [ ] Label printer or A4 printer for AWBs

    PHASE 2: SOFT LAUNCH (Weeks 3-4)¶

    Week 3: Product Listing & Pricing¶

    Day 15-17: Photography Marathon¶

    Photo Checklist Per Product: - [ ] Photo 1 - Hero: Product at 45-degree angle, full view - [ ] Photo 2 - Packaging: Box/packaging if branded - [ ] Photo 3 - Detail: Close-up of part numbers, logos - [ ] Photo 4 - Scale: Product next to ruler/common object - [ ] Photo 5 - Compatibility: If multiple fitments, show chart - [ ] Photo 6 - Installation: Context shot (where it goes) - [ ] Photo 7 - Comparison: With OEM or inferior product

    Production Target: 30-50 products photographed this week

    Day 18-19: Title Writing Formula¶

    Template: [BRAND] [PART TYPE] for [VEHICLE MODEL] [YEAR] - [KEY FEATURE] - [SKU]

    Examples: - ✅ "Filter Oli NGK for Hino J08E 2015-2019 - Original - T12345" - ❌ "Filter motor bagus murah" (too vague) - ✅ "Kampas Rem Brake Pad Ferodo for Isuzu NMR81 - Asli - TKD-787"

    SEO Keywords to Include: - [ ] Brand name (NGK, Bosch, Denso, etc.) - [ ] Part type (filter oli, busi, kampas rem, etc.) - [ ] Vehicle model compatibility - [ ] Engine codes (4HK1, 2KD, J08E) - [ ] Application (truck, bus, commercial vehicle)

    Day 20-21: Description Writing¶

    Required Sections: - [ ] Opening: Problem statement + solution - [ ] Compatibility: Full vehicle list (exact years/models) - [ ] Specifications: Dimensions, material, country of origin - [ ] Installation: Basic instructions or professional recommendation - [ ] Warranty: Terms and duration - [ ] Authenticity: How to verify genuine product - [ ] FAQ: 3-5 common questions answered

    Day 22-21: Pricing Strategy¶

    Pricing Formula: 

    LANDED COST = Purchase Price + Shipping In + Storage Cost per Unit
PLATFORM FEE = (Sales Price × Commission %) + Payment Fee
SHIPPING = Average shipping cost to customer
PROFIT MARGIN = Desired % (30-50% for parts)

FINAL PRICE = (LANDED COST + SHIPPING) ÷ (1 - PLATFORM FEE% - PROFIT MARGIN%)

    Example - Oil Filter: - Purchase: Rp 80,000 - Shipping In: Rp 5,000 - Storage: Rp 2,000 - Platform fee: 5% (TikTok) + 2% payment = 7% - Shipping Out: Rp 15,000 - Desired margin: 40%

    Calculation: - Costs: 80+5+2+15 = Rp 102,000 - Formula: 102,000 ÷ (1 - 0.07 - 0.40) = Rp 102,000 ÷ 0.53 = Rp 192,453 - Round up: Rp 200,000

    Competitive Pricing Check: - [ ] Search Tokopedia for same part - price 5-10% below market average for launch - [ ] Check Shopee prices - match or slightly lower - [ ] Factor in "free shipping" - add Rp 15,000-25,000 to price if offering free shipping - [ ] Bundle pricing: "Buy 3 Get 1 Free" calculate true cost per unit
