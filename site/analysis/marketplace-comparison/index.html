    • Standard commission rates (4.5% - 6.5%)
    • Basic seller tools

    1.6 Pros and Cons for Commercial Parts Sellers¶

    PROS ✓¶

    1. Largest Buyer Base: 100M+ monthly active users
    2. B2B Features: Corporate purchasing, bulk orders supported
    3. Trust System: Power Merchant badges increase buyer confidence
    4. Fleet Manager Reach: Many fleet operators use Tokopedia for procurement
    5. Brand Recognition: Most trusted e-commerce in Indonesia
    6. Payment Options: Corporate invoicing available for registered businesses
    7. Analytics: Detailed sales and performance metrics
    8. Cross-selling: Related product recommendations

    CONS ✗¶

    1. High Competition: 300-500 sellers per category
    2. Commission Fees: 4-6% reduces margins on low-cost parts
    3. Price War: Automatic price comparison drives prices down
    4. Storage Costs: Power Merchant requires fast shipping (need warehouse)
    5. Returns: High return rates for misidentified parts
    6. TopAds Required: Organic reach declining, paid ads necessary
    7. Customer Service: 24/7 chat response expected

    6.3 How to Manage Inventory Across Multiple Platforms¶

    Option 1: Manual Management (Low Volume)¶

    • Use spreadsheet to track stock
    • Manual update each platform
    • Suitable for < 100 SKUs
    • Cost: Rp 0 (free)

    Option 2: Inventory Management Software (Medium Volume)¶

    • Use software like SIRCLO, Ginee, or Sellcentre
    • Sync inventory across platforms
    • Automated stock updates
    • Suitable for 100-1,000 SKUs
    • Cost: Rp 500,000 - 2,000,000/month

    Option 3: ERP Integration (High Volume)¶

    • Use Odoo, Zoho, or custom ERP
    • Full inventory, accounting, CRM
    • Suitable for 1,000+ SKUs
    • Cost: Rp 5,000,000 - 20,000,000/month

    Recommended Stack:

    Volume Inventory Tool Cost Platform Integration
    Startup (< Rp 50M/month) Google Sheets + Manual Free Manual
    Growing (Rp 50-200M/month) SIRCLO or Ginee Rp 500K-1M/month Tokopedia, Shopee
    Scale (Rp 200M-1B/month) Sellcentre Pro Rp 2M-5M/month All platforms
    Enterprise (> Rp 1B/month) Custom ERP Rp 10M+/month Full integration
