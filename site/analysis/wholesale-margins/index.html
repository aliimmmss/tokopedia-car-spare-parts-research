    WHOLESALE PRICING & PROFIT MARGINS ANALYSIS¶

    Commercial Vehicle Spare Parts | Indonesia | April 2026¶

    Research Date: April 7, 2026
    Focus: Commercial Vehicle Parts (Trucks, Buses)
    Data Source: Tokopedia marketplace analysis, industry estimates

    SECTION 1: TYPICAL WHOLESALE vs RETAIL MARGINS¶

    1.1 Supply Chain Price Tiers¶

    The Indonesian automotive parts supply chain typically has 4-5 price tiers:

    IMPORTER/PRINCIPAL (Cost + 10-15%)
        ↓
MASTER DISTRIBUTOR (Cost + 20-30%)
        ↓
REGIONAL WHOLESALER (Cost + 35-50%)
        ↓
RETAILER/SHOP (Cost + 60-100%)
        ↓
END CONSUMER

    2.4 Seasonal Pricing Variations¶

    Season Price Trend Reasoning
    Jan-Mar (Rainy Season) Stable to +5% Less maintenance, slower sales
    Apr-Jun (Pre-Harvest) +10-15% Increased demand from logistics
    Jul-Sep (Dry Season) +5-10% Higher vehicle usage, more breakdowns
    Oct-Dec (Year End) +15-20% Budget clearing, fleet maintenance

    Best Buying Months: - January-February: Slowest sales, best negotiation leverage - Post-Lebaran (May-June): Many promotions

    Highest Price Months: - September-October: Peak logistics season - December: Year-end fleet maintenance rush

    3.3 Why the Price Differences?¶

    Offline Cheaper (Consumables): - No platform commission (4-6% Tokopedia/Shopee) - Cash transactions (no payment gateway fees) - Bulk purchasing power - Direct negotiation (price flexibility) - No shipping costs for local buyers

    Online Cheaper (Specialized Parts): - Wider seller competition (price comparison) - Direct from distributor (skip middleman) - Lower overhead (no physical shop) - Promotion/voucher discounts - Free shipping subsidies

    SECTION 6: KEY FINDINGS & RECOMMENDATIONS¶

    6.1 Summary: Where to Make Money¶

    BEST PROFIT MARGINS: 1. Brake Parts (80-117% margin) - High volume, moderate competition 2. Electrical Parts (70-96% margin) - Technical expertise required, low competition 3. Filters (67-87% margin) - Highest volume, easiest entry

    BEST REVENUE POTENTIAL: 1. Engine Parts (Rp 2-3.5M per sale) - High value, loyal customers 2. Electrical Parts (Rp 1.5-2.5M per sale) - Good balance of value and volume 3. Brake Parts (Rp 450K-1M per sale) - High volume compensates lower price

    BEST ENTRY POINT FOR NEW SELLERS: → Start with FILTERS (highest volume, easiest to source, moderate competition) → Add BRAKE PARTS (good margins, high volume, established demand) → Add ELECTRICAL (high margins, differentiation opportunity)
