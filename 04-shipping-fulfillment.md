# Shipping & Fulfillment

## 4.1 Weight Categories & Couriers
| Weight | Recommended Courier |
|--------|---------------------|
| <<  1 kg | JNE Reg, J&T, SiCepat |
| 1-5 kg | JNE, J&T, Ninja XL |
| 5-10 kg | JNE JTR, SiCepat GESIT |
| 10-30 kg | JNE JTR, Wahana, POS |
| > 30 kg | JNE JTR, Cargo services |

## 4.2 Packaging Requirements
- **Brake Pads:** 2+ layers bubble wrap + sturdy cardboard box.
- **Headlights:** Double-box method + "FRAGILE" stickers.
- **Spark Plugs:** Protect ceramic tips; keep in original packaging.
- **Suspension:** Wrap chrome finishes to prevent scratches.

## 4.3 Insurance
- Recommended for items > Rp 500,000.
- Required for high-value fragile items (Headlights).
