# 📦 Shipping & Fulfillment Logistics

Automotive parts present a unique logistical challenge: they range from a 50g spark plug to a 20kg shock absorber. Incorrect shipping setup leads to "Shipping Weight Discrepancies" and lost profits.

## 4.1 Weight & Volume Calculation
Tokopedia uses **Volumetric Weight** for bulky items. 
**Formula:** `(Length x Width x Height) / 6000`

**Practical Example:** A headlight assembly might weigh 2kg, but its box is 40x30x20cm.
`Calculation: (40*30*20)/6000 = 4kg`. You will be charged for 4kg, not 2kg.

## 4.2 Courier Selection Matrix

| Item Type | Weight | Recommended Courier | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
|| **Small Consumables** | < 2 kg | J&T, SiCepat | Fast and cheap for small items. |
| **Medium Parts** | 2-10kg | JNE REG, AnterAja | Balanced speed and cost. |
| **Heavy/Bulky** | 10-50kg | **JNE JTR (Trucking)** | Significantly cheaper than Regular for heavy items. |
| **Extreme/Oversize** | > 50kg | **Cargo / Wahana** | Specialized in heavy machinery/parts. |

**Pro Tip:** Always enable **Sicepat GESIT** and **JNE JTR**. Without these, your shipping cost for a set of suspension springs will be prohibitively expensive, killing your conversion rate.

## 4.3 Specialized Packaging Instructions
Automotive parts are often heavy and made of metal or glass. Standard bubble wrap is not enough.

### 🛡️ The "Zero Damage" Protocol:
1. **Brake Pads:** Wrap in bubble wrap → Place in a tight-fitting cardboard box → Fill gaps with crumpled paper to prevent shifting.
2. **Headlights/Lamps:** 
   - **Inner layer:** Soft foam wrap.
   - **Middle layer:** Heavy-duty bubble wrap.
   - **Outer layer:** "Double-Boxing" (put the product box inside a larger box with 3cm of polystyrene/styrofoam peanuts on all sides).
   - **Label:** "Fragile/Barang Pecah Belah" on all sides.
3. **Spark Plugs:** Must stay in their original plastic/cardboard trays. Ceramic insulators break easily under pressure.
4. **Suspension/Metal Parts:** Use "Stretch Film" (plastic wrap) first to prevent grease from leaking into the box, then wrap in cardboard.

## 4.4 Fulfillment Centers & Warehousing
As you scale, managing a home garage becomes impossible.

### Options for Scaling:
- **Self-Fulfillment:** Full control, but labor-intensive.
- **Dilayani Tokopedia (Fulfilled by Tokopedia):** 
   - You send stock to Tokopedia's warehouse.
   - They handle picking, packing, and shipping.
   - **Benefit:** "Dilayani Tokopedia" badge increases buyer trust and search ranking.
   - **Requirement:** High-velocity items only (don't send slow-moving parts).

## 4.5 Shipping Insurance & Claims
In the auto parts world, damage is common.
- **Mandatory Insurance:** For any item > Rp 500.000.
- **Claim Process:** 
  1. Buyer must record an **Unboxing Video** (this is a standard Tokopedia requirement).
  2. Take photos of the outer box *before* opening.
  3. Submit claim within 24 hours.
