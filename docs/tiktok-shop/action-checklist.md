#### Day 22-21: Pricing Strategy

**Pricing Formula:**
```
LANDED COST = Purchase Price + Shipping In + Storage Cost per Unit
PLATFORM FEE = (Sales Price × Commission %) + Payment Fee
SHIPPING = Average shipping cost to customer
PROFIT MARGIN = Desired % (30-50% for parts)

FINAL PRICE = (LANDED COST + SHIPPING) ÷ (1 - PLATFORM FEE% - PROFIT MARGIN%)
```

**Example - Oil Filter:**
- Purchase: Rp 80,000
- Shipping In: Rp 5,000
- Storage: Rp 2,000
- Platform fee: 5% (TikTok) + 2% payment = 7%
- Shipping Out: Rp 15,000
- Desired margin: 40%

Calculation:
- Costs: 80+5+2+15 = Rp 102,000
- Formula: 102,000 ÷ (1 - 0.07 - 0.40) = Rp 102,000 ÷ 0.53 = **Rp 192,453**
- Round up: **Rp 200,000**

**Competitive Pricing Check:**
- [ ] Search Tokopedia for same part - price 5-10% below market average for launch
- [ ] Check Shopee prices - match or slightly lower
- [ ] Factor in "free shipping" - add Rp 15,000-25,000 to price if offering free shipping
- [ ] Bundle pricing: "Buy 3 Get 1 Free" calculate true cost per unit
