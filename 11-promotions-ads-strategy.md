## 1. PROMOTION TYPES AVAILABLE

### A. TopAds (Paid Advertising)

TopAds is Tokopedia's paid advertising platform for boosting product visibility.

#### TopAds Types

| Type | Placement | Best For | Cost Model |
|------|-----------|----------|------------|
| **Iklan Produk** | Multi-placement (Beranda, Kotak Masuk, Keranjang, Halaman Produk, Hasil Pencarian) | Product visibility | CPC (Cost Per Click) |
| **Iklan Kata Kunci** | Search results page | Keyword targeting | CPC |
| **Iklan Toko / Headline** | Top banner on search results | Brand awareness | CPC |
| **Iklan Otomatis** | Multi-placement | Beginners (hands-off) | Daily budget |
| **Iklan Tanpa Modal** | Various | Risk-free advertising | Cost per SALE |
| **Tokopedia Display Network** | Banner placements | Official Store & Power Merchant PRO | CPM/CPC |

#### Cost Structure

| Metric | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| **Minimum CPC (bidding)** | Rp 250 per klik |
| **Minimum CPC per product** | Rp 300 - Rp 350 per klik |
| **Minimum remaining credit to activate** | Rp 500 |
| **Recommended daily budget** | Varies by competition |

#### How TopAds Works

1. **Set Budget** — Daily budget or total campaign budget
2. **Choose Products** — Select products to advertise
3. **Set Bidding** — Manual bidding or automatic optimization
4. **Target Keywords** — Add keywords for search visibility
5. **Pay Per Click** — Only charged when buyer clicks

### B. GMV Max (Automated Ads)

**GMV Max** = Automated advertising solution for Tokopedia & TikTok Shop.

#### Key Features

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| **Fully Automatic** | No manual ad creation — uses existing product photos, descriptions, prices |
| **ROI-Focused** | Prioritizes achieving Return on Investment targets |
| **Real-Time Optimization** | Continuously adjusts bidding, placement, targeting |
| **Multi-Platform** | Works on both Tokopedia and TikTok Shop |

#### How GMV Max Works

1. **Reads Product Details** — Pulls photos, descriptions, prices, stock from Seller Center
2. **Creates Ads Automatically** — Generates ad content without manual input
3. **Optimizes Bidding** — Handles bidding strategies in real-time
4. **Adjusts for Performance** — Fine-tunes based on market conditions

#### Benefits

- **Save Time & Effort** — No manual ad management
- **Maximize GMV** — One-click activation, algorithm handles growth
- **No Extra Creative Required** — Uses existing product assets
- **Higher ROI** — Precise targeting to purchase-ready audiences

### C. Promotion Days (GMV Max Feature)

**Promotion Days** = Automatic budget and ROI adjustments during peak shopping periods.

| Adjustment | Default Setting |
|------------|-----------------|
| **ROI Reduction** | 10%, 20%, or 30% lower during promotions |
| **Budget Increase** | +50% (customizable, max 10 increases) |
| **Timing** | Follow platform calendar or custom dates |

#### Benefits

- **Boost GMV** — Capture incremental sales during mega events (Double Days, Paydays)
- **Automate Adjustments** — No manual campaign updates needed
- **Flexible Scheduling** — Use default calendar or customize

#### Best Practices

- Enable **3-5 days before event** for system optimization
- Set **realistic ROI goals** based on recent performance
- Ensure **sufficient budget** to capture extra traffic
- **Refresh creatives** before high-traffic days

### F. WIB Ads (Waktu Indonesia Belanja)

**WIB Ads** = Peak hour targeting for maximum visibility.

#### What is WIB?

- **WIB** = Waktu Indonesia Belanja (Indonesian Shopping Time)
- Refers to **peak shopping hours** in Indonesia
- Typically **lunch breaks** and **evenings**

#### Key Benefits

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Increased Visibility** | Products appear at top of search during peak hours |
| **Targeted Reach** | Focus on high-engagement timeframes |
| **Higher Conversion** | Target buyers in purchasing mindset |
| **Competitive Edge** | Many sellers underutilize this feature |

#### Best Hours for Automotive Parts

| Time Slot | Buyer Behavior | Recommendation |
|-----------|----------------|----------------|
| **07:00 - 09:00** | Morning commute, pre-work browsing | Good for maintenance items |
| **12:00 - 14:00** | Lunch break shopping | HIGH traffic |
| **18:00 - 21:00** | Post-work browsing | HIGHEST traffic |
| **21:00 - 23:00** | Late-night impulse | Medium traffic |

## 2. TOPADS DEEP DIVE

|--------|--------|
| **Keyword Competition** | Popular keywords = higher CPC |
| **Category Competition** | Fashion/gadgets > automotive |
| **Ad Quality Score** | Better CTR = lower CPC |

| **Seasonality** | Sale periods = higher competition |

|----------|-------------|-------|

| **Specialized Parts** (EURO 4, injectors) | 5-10x | Less competition, high value |
| **Premium Brands** (BOSCH, DENSO) | 4-7x | Brand recognition helps CTR |
| **Accessories** (bulbs, wipers) | 2-4x | High competition, impulse buys |

## 7. TOKOPEDIA x TIKTOK SHOP INTEGRATION

### How Integration Works

#### Shop Mirroring Process

| Step | Process |
|------|---------|
| 1 | Bind Tokopedia account with TikTok Shop |
| 2 | Confirm integration via [integration confirmation page](https://seller-id.tokopedia.com/profile/toko-integration) |
| 3 | Products automatically copied from Tokopedia to Seller Center |
| 4 | Unified shop name across both platforms |
| 5 | Orders managed from single Seller Center |

### What Syncs Between Platforms

| Data Type | Sync Status |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Product Listings** | ✅ Automatically copied |
| **Stock/Inventory** | ✅ Real-time sync |
| **Prices** | ✅ Synced (can customize per platform) |
| **Orders** | ✅ Unified in Seller Center |
| **Shop Name** | ✅ Unified (one name for both) |
| **Reviews** | ❌ Separate per platform |
| **Ratings** | ❌ Separate per platform |

### Key Integration Notes

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Suspended Products** | Not copied — contact support to add |
| **Pre-Integration Orders** | Manage in old Tokopedia dashboard until processed |
| **Verification Time** | Up to 24 hours for shop verification |
| **Order Integration** | Up to 48 hours for Tokopedia orders |

### TikTok Shop Buyer Visibility

**Question:** Can TikTok buyers see Tokopedia stock?

**Answer:** **YES** — After integration:
- Inventory is synced in real-time
- If product sells out on Tokopedia, TikTok listing auto-updates
- Stock updates bidirectionally
- Seller can manage all inventory from unified Seller Center

### Integration Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Seller Center Account** | Active Tokopedia seller account |
| **TikTok Shop Account** | Linked to same entity |
| **Bank Account** | Single withdrawal account for both platforms |
| **Tax ID** | Confirmed in integrated Seller Center |

## 8. COMMISSION FEES BY CATEGORY

### Fee Structure Components

| Fee Type | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Platform Commission Fee** | Per successful order, varies by category |
| **Mall Service Fee** | 1.8% for Mall sellers (capped at Rp 50,000) |
| **Dynamic Commission Fee** | 4-6% additional (capped at Rp 40,000) |
| **Order Processing Fee** | Fixed Rp 1,250 per order (from Aug 11, 2025) |
| **Pre-Order Service Fee** | Additional 3% for pre-order products |

### Automotive & Motorcycle Commission Rates

| Category | Marketplace Rate | Mall Rate |
|----------|------------------|-----------|
| **Auto Replacement Parts** (most) | 7.50% | 8.50% |
| **Motorcycle Parts** (most) | 7.50-10.00% | 8.50% |
| **Car Lights & Accessories** | 7.50% | 8.50% |
| **Car Electronics** | 10.00%* | 8.50% |
| **Motorcycles** (whole vehicle) | 5.50% | 4.00% |

> *Categories at 10% get 20% discount = **effective 8%**

### Commission Rate Summary

| Rate Level | Categories |
|------------|------------|
| **1-4.25%** | Virtual products, computers, phones, jewelry |
| **5.25-7.50%** | Home appliances, food staples, health, automotive parts |
| **8-10%** | Fashion, accessories, furniture, most retail |

### Fee Calculation Example

**Product: Oil Filter (Marketplace Seller)**

| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Original Price | Rp 100,000 |
| Seller Discount | Rp 10,000 |
| Price After Discount | Rp 90,000 |
| Platform Commission (7.5%) | Rp 6,750 |
| Dynamic Commission (4%) | Rp 3,600 (capped at Rp 40,000) |
| Order Processing Fee | Rp 1,250 |
| **Total Fees** | **Rp 11,600** |
| **Seller Receives** | **Rp 78,400** |

### Fee Reduction Opportunities

| Condition | Benefit |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sell via LIVE** | Lower commission rates |
| **GMV Max spend > 1% of GMV** | Lower rates for qualifying categories |
| **GMV Max spend > 3% of GMV** | Lower rates for ALL products store-wide |
