# Daily Checklist

Daily operational tasks for running the spare parts business.

## Morning (Start of Day)

### Marketplace Check (15 min)
- [ ] Check overnight orders on Tokopedia
- [ ] Check overnight orders on Shopee  
- [ ] Check TikTok Shop messages/orders
- [ ] Respond to customer inquiries (response time target: <1 hour)

### Inventory Check (10 min)
- [ ] Check stock levels for top 20 SKUs
- [ ] Flag items below 20 units for reorder
- [ ] Update "Out of Stock" status on listings if needed

## Mid-Day (Afternoon)

### Fulfillment (30–60 min)
- [ ] Pack orders from overnight/morning
- [ ] Print shipping labels
- [ ] Hand over to courier/pickup point
- [ ] Update tracking numbers on platforms

### Price Check (10 min)
- [ ] Spot-check 5 competitor prices
- [ ] Adjust pricing if gap >15%
- [ ] Update any promotional pricing

## Evening (End of Day)

### Admin (15 min)
- [ ] Confirm all shipped orders updated
- [ ] Record sales in tracking sheet
- [ ] Check payment status (confirm received)

### B2B Follow-up (15 min)
- [ ] WhatsApp fleet managers with pending quotes
- [ ] Check payment due dates
- [ ] Follow up on overdue payments (>30 days)

## Weekly Tasks

| Day | Task | Time |
|-----|------|------|
| Monday | Competitor price audit | 30 min |
| Tuesday | Inventory reorder planning | 30 min |
| Wednesday | New product research | 1 hour |
| Thursday | B2B customer calls/visits | 2 hours |
| Friday | Financial review, cash flow | 1 hour |
| Saturday | Content creation (TikTok) | 2 hours |
| Sunday | Rest OR catch-up | Flexible |

## Monthly Tasks

- **Week 1**: Review pricing strategy, update wiki
- **Week 2**: Supplier check-in, negotiate terms
- **Week 3**: Competitor deep dive (new entrants)
- **Week 4**: Financial reconciliation, tax prep

## Emergency Triggers

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Stock <10 units on fast mover | Immediate reorder |
| Competitor drops price >20% | Evaluate match or hold |
| Negative review posted | Respond within 2 hours |
| B2B payment overdue >45 days | Pause credit, demand COD |
| Courier issues reported | Switch courier, inform customers |
