## Key Insights

### 1. No Genuine Isuzu Presence
- Zero listings at dealer price (Rp 320K)
- All sellers are aftermarket/universal
- "NATAM ISUZU GENUINE PART" claims genuine at Rp 75K (75% below dealer) - **verify authenticity**

### 2. Part Number Confusion
- Sellers use "927" interchangeably (refers to size, not part I8-97309 927-0)
- Original part number rarely mentioned
- Buyers search by vehicle + "filter oli"

### 3. Geography Clustering
- 80% of sellers in **Jakarta Pusat**
- Suggests wholesale sourcing from same suppliers
- Shipping cost advantage for Jabodetabek buyers

### 4. Volume Patterns
- **Rp 40-48K**: 500+ units sold (era jaya)
- **Rp 50-55K**: 100+ units sold (mid-market sweet spot)
- **Rp 60-77K**: <50 units sold (niche premium)
