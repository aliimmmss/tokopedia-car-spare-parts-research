## Customer Segments

### Workshop Owner (Bengkel)

**Profile**:
- Male, 35-55 years
- 5-20 years in business
- Owns 1-3 workshop locations
- Employs 2-10 mechanics

**Pain Points**:
- Unreliable parts quality
- Late deliveries = unhappy customers
- Stock running out mid-job
- Hard to find rare parts

**Buying Behavior**:
- Loyal once trust established
- Values consistency over lowest price
- Needs credit terms (14-30 days)
- Orders based on repair schedules

**Decision Criteria**:
1. Quality (won't risk comeback)
2. Availability (stock ready)
3. Price (margin preservation)
4. Delivery speed

**Quote**: *"Saya bisa beli lebih mahal, yang penting cocok dan tidak balik complain."*

---

### Fleet Manager

**Profile**:
- Male, 30-50 years
- Manages 10-100+ vehicles
- Logistics, mining, or rental company

**Top Logistics Companies:**
| Rank | Company | Fleet Size | Annual Parts Spend |
|------|---------|------------|-------------------|
| 1 | PT SiCepat Express | 2,000+ | Rp 80-120 billion |
| 2 | PT JNE Express | 1,500+ | Rp 60-100 billion |
| 3 | PT Nippon Express | 1,000+ | Rp 50-100 billion |
| 4 | PT Kamadjaja Logistics | 800+ | Rp 40-80 billion |

**Pain Points**:
- Vehicle downtime = lost revenue
- Parts not meeting fleet standards
- Documentation for audits
- Budget overruns

**Buying Behavior**:
- Bulk orders (10-50+ units)
- Scheduled maintenance purchases
- Compares 3-4 suppliers
- Needs official invoices

**Decision Criteria**:
1. Total cost of ownership
2. OEM/original quality
3. Bulk pricing tiers
4. Warranty and returns
5. Payment terms

**Quote**: *"Kalau ada warranty dan harga bulk kompetitif, saya akan repeat order."*

---

### Procurement Officer (Corporate/BUMN)

**Profile**:
- Corporate, mid-large companies
- Manages vendor relationships
- KPI: cost savings + vendor reliability
- 28-45 years old

**Top Mining Companies:**
| Company | Location | Fleet Size | Annual Parts Budget |
|---------|----------|------------|---------------------|
| PT Kaltim Prima Coal | East Kalimantan | 200+ dump trucks | Rp 100-300M per truck/year |
| PT Adaro Indonesia | South Kalimantan | 150+ dump trucks | Very High |
| PT Freeport Indonesia | Papua | 300+ heavy trucks | Very High |

**Pain Points**:
- Finding competitive quotes
- Vendor delays
- Overspending budget
- Audit/documentation issues

**Buying Behavior**:
- Formal quotation process
- Needs SOP compliance
- Evaluates multiple vendors
- Long-term relationship focus

**Decision Criteria**:
1. Compliance (certifications)
2. Pricing (tender-based)
3. Track record
4. Location/proximity

## Sales Approach by Segment

### Workshop
- Visit personally, bring samples
- Start COD, offer terms after trust
- Technical support (fitment help)
- WhatsApp for quick orders

### Fleet Manager
- Documented warranty terms
- Bulk price sheet ready
- MOQ flexibility
- Regular check-in calls

### Procurement
- Formal proposal format
- Company profile ready
- References from similar companies
- Annual contract potential

## Seasonal Insights

**Peak Demand Periods:**
- Pre-Lebaran (March–April): Rush logistics
- Post-harvest (September–October): Palm oil transport
- Q4: Year-end budget spending

**Plantation Fleets (Palm Oil):**
- Peak: April–October (harvest season)
- Companies: Astra Agro, Wilmar, Musim Mas
- Remote locations need reliable supply
