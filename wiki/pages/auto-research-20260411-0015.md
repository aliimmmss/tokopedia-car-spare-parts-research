## 🏭 COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE ANALYSIS

### Chinese Truck Import Impact

**From Tribun Otomotif (Jan 2026):**
- Pabrik Hino utilization: **Only 25%** (down from 75,000 unit capacity)
- Importir truk China masuk tanpa uji tipe & regulasi standar
- Some Chinese trucks marketed as **Euro 2** (vs Euro 4 requirement)
- Truk China masuk bentuk **CBU (Complete Built Up)** with body — bypassing local karoseri

**Strategic Implications for Parts Sellers:**
- Chinese truck owners struggle to find spare parts (limited availability)
- **OPPORTUNITY:** Stock compatible parts for Chinese brands (Shacman, Sinotruk, Foton)
- Workshop demand for "universal" parts increasing (filters, kampas rem)
