## Registration Requirements

### Documents Needed (Prepare These Now)

| Document | Tokopedia | TikTok Shop | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------------|-------|
| KTP (Identity) | Required | Required | Director/owner |
| NPWP | Required | Required | Personal or company |
| Business License (NIB) | Recommended | Recommended | For business account |
| Power Bill | Sometimes | Sometimes | Address verification |
| Bank Account | Required | Required | For settlements |
| Selfie with KTP | Required | Required | Identity confirmation |

### Registration Steps

#### Tokopedia
1. Go to https://shop-id.tokopedia.com/merchant/id
2. Fill personal/business details
3. Upload documents
4. Wait verification (1-2 business days)
5. Create first product listing

#### TikTok Shop
1. Go to https://seller-id.tokopedia.com (unified registration)
2. Link with existing Tokopedia account
3. Complete TikTok-specific requirements
4. Longer verification (3-7 days)
5. Start selling

## Fees Structure (Complete Breakdown)

### Tokopedia Fees

| Fee Type | Percentage | Applies To |
|----------|------------|------------|
| **Commission** | 5.5-7% | Product category dependent |
| **Payment Processing** | 1.5-2% | All transactions |
| **Shipping (Buyer Pays)** | - | Calculated at checkout |
| **Withdrawal Fee** | Rp 4.000-10.000 | To bank account |

**Effective Cost:** 7-9% of transaction value

### TikTok Shop Fees

| Fee Type | Percentage | Applies To |
|----------|------------|------------|
| **Commission** | 5-8% | Category dependent |
| **Payment Processing** | 1.8% | Fixed rate |
| **Shipping Subsidy** | Partial | Platform contributes |
| **Withdrawal Fee** | Rp 4.000-10.000 | To bank account |

**Effective Cost:** 8-14% of transaction value

### Fee Comparison Summary

| Platform | Commission | Payment Fee | **Total** | Best Margin Target |
|----------|------------|-------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Tokopedia | 6.5% (avg) | 1.6% | **8.1%** | 35%+ markup |
| TikTok Shop | 7.5% (avg) | 1.8% | **9.3%** | 40%+ markup |

## Policies and Compliance

### Critical Policies (Violations = Store Suspended)

| Policy | What It Means | Penalty |
|--------|---------------|---------|
| **Prohibited Products** | No counterfeits, hazardous, or illegal parts | Permanent ban |
| **Shipping Time** | Must ship within 24-48 hours of order | Late shipment penalty |
| **Returns** | Must accept legitimate returns | Dispute if refused |
| **Counterfeit** | No fake brands (OEM critical for parts) | Permanent ban |
| **Chat Response** | Respond to inquiries within target time | Performance penalty |

### Store Rating Factors

Tokopedia and TikTok track:

| Factor | Weight | How to Maintain |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| **Response Rate** | High | Answer chats within 1 hour |
| **On-Time Shipment** | Very High | Ship within 24 hours |
| **fulfillment Rate** | High | Stock accurate, no cancellations |
| **Positive Reviews** | Very High | Target 4.8+ stars |
| **Return Rate** | Medium | Keep returns <5% |

### Rating Thresholds

| Rating | Status | Action Required |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| 4.8 - 5.0 | Excellent | Maintain |
| 4.5 - 4.7 | Good | Monitor closely |
| 4.0 - 4.4 | Warning | Address issues |
| Below 4.0 | At Risk | Immediate intervention |

## Shipping Integration

### Supported Couriers

| Courier | Tokopedia | TikTok Shop | Coverage | Price Range |
|---------|-----------|-------------|----------|-------------|
| **JNE** | Yes | Yes | National | Rp 12.000-45.000 |
| **J&T Express** | Yes | Yes | National | Rp 10.000-40.000 |
| **SiCepat** | Yes | Yes | National | Rp 10.000-35.000 |
| **Ninja Xpress** | Limited | Yes | Urban | Rp 8.000-25.000 |
| **ID Express** | Yes | Limited | National | Rp 8.000-30.000 |
| **Grab** | No | Yes | Same-day | Rp 15.000-50.000 |

### For Commercial Vehicle Parts

**Considerations:**
- Heavy parts (filters, brake) → Use JNE/J&T (reliable for 1-5kg)
- Bulky items (radiators) → Negotiate bulk rate with courier
- Urgent orders (same-day) → Grab for local delivery
- Remote areas → JNE has best coverage
