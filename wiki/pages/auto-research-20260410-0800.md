### Pricing Strategies Observed

1. **Official Store Premium:** Astra Isuzu Official prices 10–15% higher than Power Merchants, but offers 5.0 trust + genuine guarantee
2. **Import OEM Arbitrage:** Exedy-branded clutch plates sell at 200–300% markup over "import made in Japan" unbranded
3. **Volume Discounting:** Sellers offering "Hemat s.d 1% Pakai Bonus" on virtually all products — non-negotiable baseline
4. **SKU Fragmentation:** Same application split into 3–5 part numbers, creating artificial scarcity pricing
