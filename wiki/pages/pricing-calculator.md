# Pricing Calculator

Quick reference formulas and tables for pricing decisions.

## Retail Price Formula

```
Retail Price = Supplier Cost × (1 + Target Margin) ÷ (1 - Platform Fee %)
```

### Example Calculation

| Step | Value | Calculation |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Supplier Cost | Rp 100.000 | - |
| Target Margin | 30% | Rp 100.000 × 1.30 = Rp 130.000 |
| Platform Fee | 10% | Rp 130.000 ÷ 0.90 = Rp 144.444 |
| **Final Price** | **Rp 145.000** | Round to nearest 100 |

## Wholesale Price Tiers

```
Wholesale = Retail × (1 - Discount %)
```

| Quantity | Discount | Calculation (Rp 145.000 retail) |
|----------|----------|--------------------------------|
| 5-10 | 10% | Rp 130.500 |
| 11-20 | 15% | Rp 123.250 |
| 21-50 | 20% | Rp 116.000 |
| 51+ | Negotiate | Custom |

## Floor Price Rule

**Never price below:**

```
Floor = Supplier Cost × 1.15
```

This ensures minimum 15% margin after fees.
