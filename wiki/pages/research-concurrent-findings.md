### 5. TRAGA E4 vs TRAGA (Standard) Interchange Report 🔧

**Catalog Analysis:**
- TRAGA (Standard): Figure 8 - Brake system
- TRAGA E4 (Euro 4): Equivalent figure structure

**Known Differences:**
1. **Engine:** Euro 4 requires EGR and DPF systems
2. **Fuel system:** Higher pressure injection
3. **Electrical:** Additional sensors for emissions
4. **Exhaust:** DPF and SCR components

**Likely Interchangeable (Standard <-> E4):**
- ✅ Body panels
- ✅ Suspension components
- ✅ Brake system (pads, shoes)
- ✅ Basic electrical (non-emissions)
- ✅ Filters (format - but E4 may have higher spec requirements)

**Likely NOT Interchangeable:**
- ❌ Engine internal components
- ❌ Fuel system high-pressure components
- ❌ Exhaust/DPF components
- ❌ Engine-specific sensors

**Recommendation:**
Source Isuzu official interchange bulletin for definitive compatibility matrix
