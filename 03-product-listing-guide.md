# 📈 Product Listing Optimization for Auto Parts

Automotive buyers are technical. They don't search for "good brake pads"; they search for "Kampas Rem Depan Toyota Avanza 2015". Your listings must be optimized for **Technical Accuracy** and **Search Intent**.

## 3.1 The "Perfect Title" Formula
Your title is the most important factor for SEO (Search Engine Optimization).

**Formula:** `[Brand] [Part Name] [Position/Side] [Compatible Vehicle + Year] - [Key Spec/Feature]`

### Examples:
- **Brake Pads:** `Bendix Brake Pads Front Kampas Rem Depan Toyota Avanza 2012-2022 - Ceramic Low Dust`
- **Spark Plugs:** `NGK Iridium IX Spark Plug BKR6E-11 Honda Jazz GE8 GD3 Original`
- **Filters:** `Bosch Oil Filter OF-1101 Saringan Oli Toyota Avanza Rush Terios`

## 3.2 Critical Attribute Mapping
Filling out the "Product Specifications" section is not optional. It filters your product into the correct categories.

| Attribute | Why it Matters | Example Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Brand** | Core filter for buyers | `Denso`, `OEM`, `Universal` |
| **Part Number** | Technical buyers search by this | `12345-ABC-01` |
| **Compatible Model** | Prevents "Wrong Fitment" returns | `Honda Brio 2016-2023` |
| **Material** | Quality indicator | `Ceramic`, `Platinum`, `Neoprene` |
| **Position** | Essential for suspension/brakes | `Front Right (Depan Kanan)` |

## 3.3 Visual Strategy: The "Trust Gallery"
Buyers are afraid of receiving the wrong part. Your photos must eliminate that fear.

### The 7-Photo Framework:
1. **The Hero Shot:** Clean, white background, full product view.
2. **The Packaging:** Show the brand box and hologram stickers.
3. **The Part Number:** Macro shot of the embossed part number on the metal.
4. **The Scale:** Product next to a ruler or a common object (like a coin) to show size.
5. **The Fitment:** A photo of the connector or mounting point.
6. **The Comparison:** Original vs. Old worn-out part.
7. **The Compatibility Table:** A graphic listing all compatible years/models.

## 3.4 Pricing Strategy for Indonesia
The Indonesian market is highly price-sensitive.

- **The "Loss Leader" Strategy:** Price your "Fast Moving" items (Oil filters, Spark plugs) very aggressively to attract traffic.
- **The "Bundle" Strategy:** Create bundles (e.g., "Full Tune-up Kit: Spark Plugs + Oil Filter + Air Filter"). This increases your Average Order Value (AOV).
- **Psychological Pricing:** Use `Rp 149.000` instead of `Rp 150.000`.

## 3.5 SEO Tips for the Indonesian Market
Use "Keyword Stuffing" intelligently in the description, not the title.

**Keywords to include in the description:**
- *Bahasa terms:* "Suku cadang mobil", "Sparepart original", "Kualitas OEM", "Presisi", "Awet".
- *Common misspellings:* "Busi" (correct), "Bousi" (common error).
- *Car nicknames:* e.g., "Avanza Veloz", "Innova Reborn".
