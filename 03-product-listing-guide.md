# Product Listing Guide

## 3.1 Title Formula
`[Brand] [Part Name] [Compatibility] - [Key Feature]`
*Example: NGK Spark Plug BKR6E-11 - Honda Toyota Mitsubishi Original*

## 3.2 Required Attributes
- **Brand:** Use "OEM" or "Universal" if unbranded.
- **Part Number:** Critical for search ranking.
- **Compatible Vehicles:** List all models and year ranges.
- **Material:** e.g., Ceramic, Semi-metallic.

## 3.3 Photo Best Practices
- **Background:** Pure white or neutral.
- **Coverage:** Product should occupy 80% of the frame.
- **Quantity:** 5-7 photos (Main, Packaging, Logo Close-up, Part Number, Installation Diagram).

## 3.4 Pricing & SEO
- **Pricing:** Calculate (Product cost + Platform fees ~3-5% + Shipping).
- **Keywords:** Use terms like "kampas rem", "busi mobil", "filter oli" in descriptions.
