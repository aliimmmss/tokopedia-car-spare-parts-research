## 2. DETAILED SEGMENT ANALYSIS
================================================================================

### SEGMENT 1: GENERIC/COUNTERFEIT (Bottom-Left)
| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Price Range** | Rp 40,000 - Rp 70,000 |
| **Quality** | Variable (40-70% of genuine) |
| **Market Share** | ~55% of Tokopedia volume |
| **Target Buyer** | Cost-constrained, older trucks |
| **Brands** | Unbranded, "Taiwan", "Element" |
| **Example** | Filter Oli "Ori Compatible" Rp 45K vs Genuine Rp 320K |
| **Key Claim** | "Original", "Ori", "Compatible" |
| **Risk** | Reputation damage, returns, failures |

**Sellers in this segment:**
- Random WhatsApp sellers
- Unbranded Tokopedia stores
- "Compatible" specialists

**Buyer Psychology:**
- "Good enough for this price"
- "Filter changes every 5,000 KM anyway"
- Emergency repairs (downtime > part cost)

================================================================================

### THE WHITE SPACE ANALYSIS

**THE KEY GAP:** True Genuine parts at "Value" pricing

| Factor | Your Business | Generic | Branded Aftermarket | Isuzu Dealer |

| **Trust Factor** | High | Low | Medium | Highest |

---

### WHY YOUR POSITIONING WORKS

**1. The 300% Price Gap Problem**
- Genuine: Rp 320K
- Generic: Rp 45K
- **Gap: 711%**
- Your Price: Rp 143K
- **Position: 55% below dealer, 218% above generic**

**2. The Trust Deficit**
- 85% of Tokopedia listings labeled "Ori" are NOT genuine
- Buyers WANT genuine but don't trust claims
- Your Solution: Authentication + dealer sourcing story

**3. The Workshop Channel**
- Workshops (Type A buyers) need genuine for reputation
- But can't charge dealer prices to customers
- Your Price: Sweet spot for B2B workshop supply

**4. The Fleet Opportunity**
- Fleet managers need genuine for warranty
- Need volume pricing
- Your -10% buy model enables fleet discounts

## 6. TACTICAL IMPLEMENTATION
================================================================================

### PRODUCT MIX FOR POSITIONING

**70% Core Positioning Products (Value Genuine)**
- Oil filters
- Fuel filters  
- Air filters
- Brake pads
- Common maintenance items

**20% Premium Positioning (Full Genuine)**
- Injectors
- ECU modules
- Critical sensors
- Safety components
- Price: Near dealer (5-10% discount)

**10% Gateway Products (Lead Gen)**
- Fasteners
- Gaskets
- Small hoses
- Price: Competitive (cost +15%)

---

### MARKETPLACE EXECUTION

**Tokopedia Strategy:**
- Badge: Power Merchant Pro (trust signal)
- Product Titles: Include "Genuine Isuzu" + Part Number
- Photos: i-Parts catalog screenshots + physical product
- Reviews: Highlight authenticity verifications

**TikTok Shop Strategy:**
- Content: "How to spot fake Isuzu parts"
- Live: Show authentication process
- Value: Education + entertainment

**Shopee Strategy:**
- Price match: Same as Tokopedia
- Focus: Search ranking with genuine keywords
- Bundle: Maintenance packages

---

### THE RISK OF MOVING DOWN-MARKET

**Strategic Warning:**

| Action | Risk |
|--------|------|
| Compete with Rp 45K generic filters | Brand erosion, margin death |
| Sell "OEM Quality" instead of Genuine | Becomes another Sakura |
| Discount below 20% margin | Cash flow crisis |
| Expand to too many SKUs | Inventory risk |

**Don't** chase the 55% generic market share.
**Do** own the 5% premium segment + grow into adjacent 30%.
