## 1. TOKOPEDIA SELLER FEES (2024-2025)

### 1.1 Biaya Komisi (Commission Fee)

Commission is calculated as a percentage of the **product selling price** (excluding shipping).

| Category / Tier | Commission Rate | Notes |
|-----------------|-------------------|-------|
| **Regular Seller (All Categories)** | 4.5% - 6.5% | Standard rate for automotive spare parts |
| **Under Rp 500,000** | 5.5% - 6.5% | Higher rate for low-value items |
| **Rp 500,000 - Rp 2,000,000** | 4.5% - 5.5% | Mid-tier products (most common for parts) |
| **Above Rp 2,000,000** | 4.0% - 5.0% | Discount for high-value items |
| **Power Shop** | 2.5% - 4.0% | Requires 200+ orders/month, Rp 10M revenue/month |
| **Official Store (Mall)** | 1.8% (capped at Rp 50k/product) | Brand authorization required |

**Category Classification for Commercial Vehicle Parts:**
- **Main Category:** Otomotif (Automotive)
- **Subcategory:** Suku Cadang (Spare Parts)
- **Product Type:** Suku Cadang Truk, Suku Cadang Bus, Suku Cadang Alat Berat
- **Special Note:** Heavy parts (radiators, engines) are classified under "Suku Cadang Lainnya" with same commission rates

### 1.2 Biaya Layanan (Service Fee)

| Fee Type | Amount | Applied To | Notes |
|----------|--------|------------|-------|
| **Transaction Service Fee** | **Rp 0** | All transactions | Tokopedia does NOT charge flat service fee per transaction |
| **Payment Processing Fee** | 1.5% - 2.0% | Successful transactions | Deducted from payout |
| **Refund Processing Fee** | Rp 5,000 - Rp 15,000 | Buyer-initiated refunds | Deducted if refund approved |

**Comparison:** Tokopedia does NOT have a flat per-transaction service fee (unlike some other platforms). This is advantageous for low-value transactions.

### 1.3 Biaya Admin Pembayaran (Payment Processing Fees)

| Payment Method | Fee Rate | Notes |
|----------------|----------|-------|
| **Bank Transfer (Virtual Account)** | 1.5% | Most common for B2B buyers |
| **Credit Card** | 2.0% | Higher fee due to interchange |
| **e-Wallets (OVO, GoPay, ShopeePay)** | 1.5% | Popular for B2C buyers |
| **QRIS** | 1.5% - 1.7% | Cross-platform payments |
| **COD (Cash on Delivery)** | 2.0% - 2.5% | Higher risk, higher fee |
| **PayLater (Kredivo, Home Credit)** | 2.0% - 3.0% | BNPL options |

**Recommendation:** Encourage bank transfer payments for commercial buyers — lowest fee, faster settlement (T+1). COD is acceptable for new buyers but costs 0.5-1% extra.

## 5. PROFIT MARGIN CALCULATOR

### 5.1 Complete Fee Formula

**Net Profit Formula:**

```
Harga Jual (Selling Price)
- HPP (COGS - cost from supplier)
- Biaya Komisi Platform (% of Harga Jual)
- Biaya Layanan (flat/percentage)
- Biaya Admin Pembayaran (% of Harga Jual)
- Biaya Pengiriman (shipping after subsidy)
- Biaya Packaging
- Biaya TopAds (optional, allocated per order)
- Biaya Retur/Pengembalian (estimate 2-3%)
= LABA BERSIH (Net Profit)

Margin % = (Laba Bersih / Harga Jual) x 100
```

### 7.2 Pricing Strategy by Platform

**Tokopedia Strategy:**
- Price competitively against established sellers
- Aim for 30-35% gross margin minimum
- Use TopAds for high-intent keywords ("sparepart hino", "filter canter")
- Bundle complementary parts for higher AOV
- Target: Rp 300,000+ average order value

**TikTok Shop Strategy:**
- Price can be slightly higher due to discovery model
- Bundle deals work exceptionally well
- Lowest price not required — content quality matters more
- Target: Rp 200,000+ average order value
- Focus on visual/decorative parts (chrome accessories, LED lights)

### 7.3 Platform Priority Recommendation

| Business Stage | Primary Platform | Secondary Platform | Reasoning |
|----------------|------------------|-------------------|-----------|
| **Launch (0-100 orders/month)** | Tokopedia | TikTok Shop | Build credibility first |
| **Growth (100-500 orders)** | Tokopedia + TikTok | Equal focus | Diversify revenue |
| **Scale (500+ orders)** | Tokopedia (Power Shop) | TikTok Shop | Power Shop reduces fees to match TikTok |
| **B2B Focus** | Tokopedia only | — | TikTok not effective for fleet buyers |

### 7.4 Cost Optimization Tips

**Reduce Payment Processing Fees (Save 0.5-1%):**
1. Offer Rp 5,000 discount for Bank Transfer vs COD
2. Display "Transfer = Lebih Cepat Diproses" messaging
3. Get Tokopedia PayLater for integrated payment (lower fee)

**Reduce Shipping Costs (Save Rp 3,000-8,000/order):**
1. Negotiate bulk shipping rates with couriers
2. Use "Paket Agregator" — platform-negotiated rates
3. Ship from central location (Bandung is good)
4. Optimize packaging weight (avg parts 1-3kg)

**Optimize TopAds/GMV Spend (Save 20-30%):**
1. Focus on "long-tail" keywords — less competition
2. Use automatic bidding for first 30 days
3. Pause underperforming keywords quickly
4. Set daily budget caps to prevent overspend

**Reduce Return Costs:**
1. Upload fitment compatibility chart (year, model, engine)
2. Include actual product photos (not catalog)
3. Measure parts before listing — accurate dimensions
4. Accept returns gracefully — avoid negative reviews
