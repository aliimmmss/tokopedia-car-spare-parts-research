## PART D: KEY LESSONS FOR YOUR SHOP

---

### For Consumer Car Parts

1. **One-Stop Shop Strategy (Jasutra Model)**
   - Carry tires, filters, oil, bulbs, wipers, car care
   - Customers buy multiple items per transaction
   - Higher average order value (AOV)

2. **Niche Dominance Strategy (Bohlamhid Model)**
   - Own ONE category (spark plugs, filters, etc.)
   - Build brand authority
   - Offer reseller program for B2B growth

3. **OEM Authority Strategy (DENSO Model)**
   - Partner with OEM brands (DENSO, NGK, T-OPT)
   - Highlight manufacturer relationships
   - Target workshops needing genuine parts for warranty repairs

4. **Multi-Platform Presence**
   - All top sellers are on Tokopedia + Shopee + TikTok
   - Different platforms = different customer segments
   - TikTok Shop = younger buyers, Tokopedia = older/professional

5. **Vehicle Compatibility in Titles**
   - Include car models in product titles
   - Example: "Busi NGK Innova Fortuner" not just "Busi NGK"
   - Improves search visibility

---

### For Commercial Vehicle Parts

1. **Technical Accuracy is Non-Negotiable**
   - Display exact OEM part numbers
   - Include engine codes (4HK1, J08E, J08C)
   - Fleet managers search by part number, not description

2. **EURO 4/5 Specialization = Higher Margin**
   - Modern trucks require specialized parts
   - Lower competition due to complexity
   - Higher margins (Rp1.9M - Rp6.25M per item)

3. **Offer OEM + Aftermarket Options**
   - Budget customers: Sakura, Hengst, aftermarket
   - Premium customers: WABCO, BOSCH, genuine parts
   - Capture both market segments

4. **Warranty = Trust**
   - Commercial buyers need assurance for expensive parts
   - Offer warranty for OEM parts
   - "Garansi Original" in product titles

5. **Multi-Brand Coverage**
   - Don't limit to one truck brand
   - Cover HINO + Isuzu + Mitsubishi + Mercedes
   - Fleet operators have mixed fleets
