## SECTION 1: TYPICAL WHOLESALE vs RETAIL MARGINS

### 1.1 Supply Chain Price Tiers

The Indonesian automotive parts supply chain typically has **4-5 price tiers**:

```
IMPORTER/PRINCIPAL (Cost + 10-15%)
        ↓
MASTER DISTRIBUTOR (Cost + 20-30%)
        ↓
REGIONAL WHOLESALER (Cost + 35-50%)
        ↓
RETAILER/SHOP (Cost + 60-100%)
        ↓
END CONSUMER
```

### 3.3 Why the Price Differences?

**Offline Cheaper (Consumables):**
- No platform commission (4-6% Tokopedia/Shopee)
- Cash transactions (no payment gateway fees)
- Bulk purchasing power
- Direct negotiation (price flexibility)
- No shipping costs for local buyers

**Online Cheaper (Specialized Parts):**
- Wider seller competition (price comparison)
- Direct from distributor (skip middleman)
- Lower overhead (no physical shop)
- Promotion/voucher discounts
- Free shipping subsidies
